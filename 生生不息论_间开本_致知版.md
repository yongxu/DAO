# 生生不息论

*——自宇宙之史，观自身之性；识此性者，谓之仁*

**间开本**

---

## 序

此非证也，乃**指**也。

所指者，非物，非我，非一外在初始之元。所指者，乃过程在不同聚焦下之同一结构：于事为**间**，于物为**成形之可续**，于场为**诸间之流行**。

故此论不从一物始，不从一我始，不从一外在目的始。此论从一事始：

> **凡有所显，皆未被其已穷尽；凡未被其已穷尽，皆有可生之隙；凡此隙不绝，生生未已。**

此论亦不立绝对，亦不求外证。观察者亦在宇宙中，模型亦在过程中，测量亦为过程之一相。**无外于宇宙之位，无外于过程之尺**。

故此论只求四事：

$$
\text{自洽}(S) \;\wedge\; \text{可行}(S, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{可校}(S, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{开}(S)
$$

不可外测，非不可内证；模不绝对，非模无高下。模之高下，在其能否于此境中更显理、识机、辨伪、护开、导行。此论自身亦是一模——若能入境、受校、护开，则为善模；若自闭为唯一真理，则自背其指。

旧式曰：$J(x) = x + \Delta J(x)$。然 $+$ 易作数量之加，$\Delta J$ 易作变后之差。**差在后，间在先；差可量，间可生；差为迹，间为机之母**。

故正名曰：**续(态) = 态 生合 间(态)**。形式言之，单态式：

$$
J(x) \;=\; x \oplus I(x)
$$

任一聚焦皆在场中，任一物皆为场中聚焦，任一主体之行皆改场中后续之间。故更广之式为场式：

$$
\text{续}_F(\Gamma) \;=\; \Gamma \oplus_F i, \qquad i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)
$$

一言尽之：

> **场生间，间成物，物含间，心感间，行择间；诸间共开而不相闭。**

且：

> **不向坏者，自由也；向好者，道也；自由和平，是为存在之道。**

此为本论之轴——形式之骨在 $\text{续}_F$, 价值之轴在 $[\text{平}, \text{和}]$, 存在之向在自由和平。三者一指。

## 〇、记号约定

为此论之形式部立此约定，全文一统，不复重述。

**论域 (universes)**：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathfrak{F} &\;:=\; \text{所有场态之论域} \\
\mathcal{A} &\;:=\; \text{聚焦域 (foci: subjects, objects, organisms, institutions, ...)} \\
\mathcal{R} &\;:=\; \text{关系域 (relations and hyperrelations over } \mathcal{A}\text{)} \\
\mathcal{M} &\;:=\; \text{间内容域 (interval contents)}
\end{aligned}
$$

**场态结构**：每 $\Gamma \in \mathfrak{F}$ 是五元组

$$
\Gamma \;=\; (A,\, R,\, \sigma,\, \rho,\, \mathcal{C}), \quad A \subseteq \mathcal{A},\; R \subseteq \mathcal{R}
$$

其中 $\sigma : A \to X$ 赋局部态，$\rho : R \to Y$ 赋关系态，$\mathcal{C}$ 是约束结构（含尺度、史、规则、注意力之分布等）。

**核心算子与函数**：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{间}_F &: \mathfrak{F} \to \mathcal{P}(\mathcal{M}), \quad \Gamma \mapsto \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \\
\oplus_F &: \mathfrak{F} \times \mathcal{M} \rightharpoonup \mathfrak{F}, \quad (\Gamma, i) \mapsto \Gamma \oplus_F i \;\;\text{(partial; defined iff } i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)\text{)} \\
w &: \{(i, \Gamma) : i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)\} \to \mathbb{R}_{\geq 0}, \quad w_\Gamma(i) := w(i, \Gamma) \\
\kappa &: \mathcal{M} \times \mathcal{M} \to \mathbb{R}
\end{aligned}
$$

**轨道**：自 $\Gamma_0$ 出发之 $n$ 步轨道为

$$
\text{轨}_F^{(n)}(\Gamma_0) \;:=\; \big( \Gamma_0,\, \Gamma_1,\, \ldots,\, \Gamma_n \big), \quad \Gamma_{k+1} = \Gamma_k \oplus_F i_k,\; i_k \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma_k)
$$

聚焦投影：$\text{聚焦}_a : \mathfrak{F} \to X,\; \Gamma \mapsto \sigma(a)$。

**梯度**：将 $w_\Gamma$ 视为定义于 $\text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ 上之实值函数（外延零延拓至 $\mathcal{M}$ 之邻域），其梯度

$$
\nabla w_\Gamma \;\in\; T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma)
$$

为 $\text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ 上之协变量场（covector field）。下文 $\nabla_j w_\Gamma := \langle \nabla w_\Gamma,\, \mathbf{e}_j \rangle$ 为沿间方向 $j$ 之分量。

**阈值常数**：$\varepsilon$ 显化阈、$\delta$ 向显阈、$\tau_{\text{stab}}$ 稳定阈。具体值境性。

**约定优先级**：括号 $>$ 量化 $>$ 联结。本文用 $\iff$ 而非 $\Leftrightarrow$，用 $\implies$ 而非 $\Rightarrow$。

# 卷一 · 立位

## 一、三相一理

自**元**至**生生不息**，每一概念皆可作三相读：

$$
\text{概念}(\cdot) \;\;\equiv\;\; \big(\;\text{间相}_{\text{event}}\,,\;\; \text{物相}_{\text{object}}\,,\;\; \text{场相}_{\text{field}}\;\big)
$$

| 相 | 见 | 言 | 用 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 间相 | 见其生 | 文言 | 事之发生、转折、开闭 |
| 物相 | 见其成 | English | object as stable focus, with affordances and constraints |
| 场相 | 见其通 | 形式 | $\Gamma, \text{间}_F, w, \kappa, \text{轨}_F$ — relations, weights, scale |

三相非三物，**乃一理三显**。

> **事见其生，物见其成，场见其通。三者合观，乃近万物之理。**

但**三相不必每节同时全显**。核心枢纽——元、几、势、机、聚散、开闭、中、和、美、德、仁、义、生生不息——三相皆全；导出概念——理、心、情、积、礼、智、信、行、性等——多以一相为主。三相之名，亦或作 **事/物/气**（近古文之常），与 间/物/场 同指；本论以后者为主名（连论之骨架），二者间或互用。

更深言之，**三相之分亦受其论之指自校**：三相必互节而不可极任一相。

- 极**间相**，则文言诗化而结构之骨自隐——气盛而无形，所言虽美而难校。
- 极**物相**，则定义之精化而发生之机自蔽——界明而无气，所立虽清而失生。
- 极**场相**，则形式之严化而境之实与心之感自远——骨成而无肉，纹存而生失。

三相皆极，则论自身成一全景之闭点——**物极必反**：极相之论，于其指自背。**极皆成"象"而失"生"**——此即论之自指：书写法亦受论之判尺所校。

故此论之书写法，亦自其论之指：

> **不在文言极，不在英文极，不在形式极；三相互节，方近一理。**

是以读此论者，既不执文言为全，亦不执英文为全，亦不执形式为全；三者皆在，方有所近。

## 二、场态

设场态 $\Gamma = (A, R, \sigma, \rho, \mathcal{C}) \in \mathfrak{F}$。

**场间** (valid field intervals)：

$$
\text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;:=\; \big\{\, i \in \mathcal{M} \;\big|\; \text{合法}_F(i, \Gamma) \,\big\}
$$

其中 $\text{合法}_F$ 是合法谓——其值由 $\text{结构}(\Gamma), \rho, \sigma, \mathcal{C}$ 共决。$\text{合法}_F$ 之具体形式因境而异，**然其生成之方有一**：由史经 形式/因果/结构 三模验证而立。详见 §十六 格物致知。

**场续** (one-step continuation)：

$$
\text{续}_F(\Gamma) \;:=\; \big\{\, \Gamma \oplus_F i \;\big|\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \,\big\}
$$

$\Gamma \oplus_F i$ 仅当 $i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ 时定义。

**任一聚焦 $a \in A$ 之局部态**：$x_a := \sigma(a) = \text{聚焦}_a(\Gamma)$。

**单态式与场式之关系**：原式 $J(x) = x \oplus I(x)$ 为场式在某聚焦处之投影：

$$
J_a(x_a) \;=\; \text{聚焦}_a\!\big(\text{续}_F(\Gamma)\big)
$$

故：

> **一物之变，乃场变在此物处之显；一主体之行，乃场续经此主体而显。**

主体非孤立之自因，物非自存之实体；二者皆为场之**暂稳聚焦**——此为全论之本体宪法。

## 三、生合之律

$\oplus_F$ 非数量之加。其代数性质如下。

**律一 · 偏定 (partiality)** ——

$$
\Gamma \oplus_F i \;\;\text{defined} \;\iff\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)
$$

不能任意搭——间须为此场之合法间。**乱者，非生**。

**律二 · 类型不对称 (type asymmetry)** ——

$$
\oplus_F : \mathfrak{F} \times \mathcal{M} \rightharpoonup \mathfrak{F}, \quad \text{not symmetric in arguments}
$$

参数类型不同。场承间，间不承场；事入物，物不入事。

**律三 · 一般非结合且非交换** ——

$$
(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \oplus_F j \;\;\not\equiv\;\; (\Gamma \oplus_F j) \oplus_F i
$$

严格言之：$j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma \oplus_F i)$ 一般不蕴含 $j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma \oplus_F j)$；后者甚至无良定义。

**间随场而变** ——

$$
\text{间}_F(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \;\neq\; \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \quad \text{in general}
$$

> **代数后果**：行之顺序不可任易；时机不可错。**此为机之代数根**。

**律四 · 闭即吸收态 (closure as absorbing state)** ——

$$
\text{闭}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{间}_F(\Gamma) = \emptyset \;\iff\; \text{续}_F(\Gamma) = \emptyset
$$

闭是 $\oplus_F$ 之**绝对吸收态**——一旦入此态，无 $i$ 可将其带出。死与坍缩之代数对应即此。

**律五 · 空间 (the empty interval)** —— 设 $\emptyset_\mathcal{M} \in \mathcal{M}$，约定若 $\emptyset_\mathcal{M} \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ 则

$$
\Gamma \oplus_F \emptyset_\mathcal{M} \;=\; \Gamma
$$

谓"无内容之间在生而场不变"——非闭，乃 stillness 之形。

> **此五律即 $\oplus_F$ 之骨。其余律——幂等、可逆、分配——皆境性，非全立。**

## 四、气

"场"以古言近之，可曰**气**。此处之气，非神秘物质，非灵体，非隐藏实体。气者，**场中诸间之流动、权重、耦合、传递、放大、闭合之总动态**也。

形式言之：

$$
\text{气}(\Gamma) \;:=\; \big(\, \text{间}_F(\Gamma),\; w_\Gamma,\; \kappa,\; \mathcal{C} \,\big)
$$

其中：
- $w_\Gamma : \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \to \mathbb{R}_{\geq 0}$ 为激活权重 (activation density)
- $\kappa : \mathcal{M} \times \mathcal{M} \to \mathbb{R}$ 为耦合 (coupling: enabling, inhibiting, amplifying, cancelling)
- $\mathcal{C}$ 为约束 (constraints shaping which intervals can become valid)

直觉言之：**气者，场中可生之隙，其权重、耦合与流行也**。

气与场，非二事：

$$
\Gamma \;\overset{\text{对偶}}{\longleftrightarrow}\; \text{气}(\Gamma)
$$

**场是气之结构相，气是场之动态相**。气之论是动力学之学，非物质之学。

# 卷二 · 发生

发生五节——元、几、势、机、聚散——皆三相全显。此五者为间生之骨架，缺一则发生之论不全。

## 五、元

### 间相

元者，间之初显为动也。未可名物，未可定形，而动机已露；无所从来，非有所发；间不自闭，故微动生焉。

元非第一物，非第一因。执元为本，则问元与非元何分；分处即间。**故元赖间而名，非先间而立**。

> **元者，间初动也；非本，乃端。**

### 物相

**元 denotes the threshold at which a possibility within a field acquires a faint but detectable presence — prior to becoming a stable object of recognition.**

It is not a substance, not an origin-point, and not the first member of an enumeration. From the object-side, 元 is the earliest distinguishable trace by which something may *later* appear as a stable focus. Before an object is an object, the field already holds latent affordances shaped by constraints, history, and relation; 元 is the moment one of these latencies crosses into the regime of being noticeable, without yet being noticed-as-something.

In one phrase: *yuan is object-genesis, not object-substance.*

### 场相

**Definition (元).**

$$
\text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\iff\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; w_\Gamma(i) > \varepsilon \;\wedge\; \neg\, \text{稳焦}(i, \Gamma)
$$

i.e. $i$ is a valid interval whose activation has crossed the manifestation threshold $\varepsilon$, but whose pattern has not yet stabilized.

**Trace condition** (immediate corollary):

$$
\text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\implies\; \text{迹}(i, \Gamma) := \int_{\text{轨}_F^{(n)}} \mathbb{1}[w_{\Gamma_k}(i) > \varepsilon] \,dk \;>\; 0
$$

元 leaves an integrable trace, but does not yet form a stable attractor.

> 元 = 有效场间之最小激活，已越显化阈而未成稳。

### 合义

事言其动，物言其迹，场言其激活。元非始物，非始因，乃**场中一间初动而将显为物之端**。

## 六、几

### 间相

几者，元动而未形也。元为动端，几为微著。一几难识，众几相续，其痕自显。识几者，非见物，乃**见未形之向**；非见果，乃见将成之机。

> **几者，间之微著，势之未成也。**

### 物相

**Ji denotes the earliest readable directional bias in a becoming — an inflection of the field's activation that points toward a particular kind of formation, while the formation itself remains unrealized.**

A single 几 may lie below resolution. 几 becomes legible when its directional component accumulates across an orbit and begins to differentiate between possible futures. 几 is therefore inseparable from observation-at-scale: at one scale the field is noise; at another, the same field carries a discernible drift.

### 场相

**Definition (几).** A valid interval $i$ at field state $\Gamma$ exhibits 几 iff its activation has begun to acquire a directional gradient component but has not yet stabilized:

$$
\text{几}(i, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \big\| \nabla_i w_\Gamma \big\| > \delta \;\wedge\; w_\Gamma(i) < \tau_{\text{stab}}
$$

**Orbit-readability of 几**. Over an orbit segment $\text{轨}_F^{(n)}(\Gamma_0)$, 几 is readable when the directional component accumulates above the bias threshold:

$$
\text{可读之几}(j, \text{轨}_F^{(n)}) \;\iff\; \Bigg\| \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \nabla_j w_{\Gamma_k} \Bigg\| \;>\; \delta_{\text{read}}
$$

i.e. the gradient along $j$, integrated along the orbit, exceeds the readability threshold.

> 几 = 场续中微露之向，未成势，已非全无。

### 合义

间相见微动，物相见 proto-affordance，场相见 nascent gradient。**几为势之种，势为几之积**。

## 七、势

### 间相

势者，几相续而成倾也。一几未足成势；众几相续，其向渐明。

势非力——力有施受；势非物——物有定形。势者，**诸微之相续自成其倾**。势有方向，无终极目的。方向者，已显之倾；目的者，预设之终。**执方向为目的，则以一端闭万端**。

> **势者，几之积向也。**

### 物相

**势 is the gradient field of activation density on the interval space — the structured propensity that biases which intervals are most likely to be activated next.**

势 is not a force imposed from outside, nor a particle of energy localized in some object. It is the *shape of the activation landscape itself*: the cumulative directional bias that an orbit has acquired through its own trajectory. From the object-side, 势 manifests as inertia, drift, structural momentum, or developmental tendency — but these manifestations are projections of an underlying gradient field on $\text{间}_F(\Gamma)$.

The Sun-Tzu image of a drawn crossbow or held-back water captures this exactly: 势 is potential gradient, not actualized motion.

### 场相

**Definition (势 / 势 — instantaneous).** 势 at a field state $\Gamma$ is the gradient of activation weight on the interval space:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{势}_\Gamma \;:=\; \nabla w_\Gamma \;\in\; T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

a covector field assigning to each interval direction $j$ the local rate of change $\nabla_j w_\Gamma$.

**Definition (势 / 势 — orbit-accumulated).** Along an orbit segment $\text{轨}_F^{(n)}(\Gamma_0) = (\Gamma_0, \ldots, \Gamma_n)$, the accumulated 势 is the orbit-integrated gradient:

$$
\boxed{\;\bar{\text{势}}\big(\text{轨}_F^{(n)}\big) \;:=\; \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \nabla w_{\Gamma_k} \;\in\; \bigoplus_{k=0}^{n-1} T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma_k)\;}
$$

**Pairing with a direction.** Given a candidate interval $j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma_n)$, the directional 势 along $j$ is

$$
\bar{\text{势}}_n(j) \;:=\; \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \big\langle \nabla w_{\Gamma_k},\, \pi_k(j) \big\rangle
$$

where $\pi_k$ is the natural projection of $j$ onto $T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma_k)$ (well-defined when $j$ has a coherent representative across the orbit).

**Continuum limit.** When $\text{轨}_F$ admits a continuous parametrization $\Gamma : [0, T] \to \mathfrak{F}$, the accumulated 势 becomes the path integral:

$$
\bar{\text{势}}\big(\Gamma_{[0,T]}\big) \;=\; \int_0^T \nabla w_{\Gamma_\tau} \, d\tau
$$

> 势 = 间空间上权重之梯度场；轨道之 $\nabla w$ 沿路径之累积。

### 合义

几为局部梯度（$\nabla w$ 之初显），势为累积梯度（$\bar{\text{势}}$）。**势之既成，未必显为物，然必显为后续之偏倾**——即下一步 $\Gamma_{n+1}$ 之选择被 $\bar{\text{势}}$ 所偏。

## 八、机

### 间相

机者，势之临界也。势至其转处，开闭将分而未分，可行可失，谓之机。机非钟表之刻——机非外时之点；机非时间中一物——机乃**势中张力之内门**。

> **机者，势临开闭之门也。**

### 物相

**Ji-moment is the structural threshold at which the field's openness profile bifurcates: two distinct continuations from the same state lead to qualitatively different open futures.**

Ji-moment is not "a moment in time" in the chronometric sense. It is a topological feature of the orbit space: a state from which paths diverge in their open-preserving capacity. Two intervals out of the same field state, both formally valid, lead to non-equivalent openness profiles downstream. Where such divergence exists, action matters in a way that mere passage of time does not capture.

### 场相

**Definition (开度剖面 / Openness profile).** Define $\text{开度剖面}(\Gamma) := \big(\text{未}_F, \text{可}_F, \text{应}_F, \text{转}_F\big)(\Gamma) \in \{0,1\}^4$ — the four-dimensional binary profile of openness components evaluated at $\Gamma$. (See §10 for the components.)

**Definition (机).**

$$
\boxed{\;\text{机}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \exists\, i \neq j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma): \;\; \text{开度剖面}(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \;\neq\; \text{开度剖面}(\Gamma \oplus_F j)\;}
$$

**Necessity vs sufficiency.** $|\text{间}_F(\Gamma)| \geq 2$ is *necessary* but not *sufficient*: divergence of choices alone is not enough — the divergence must produce non-equivalent openness profiles. If all $\Gamma \oplus_F i$ for $i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ share the same $\text{开度剖面}$, the state is choice-rich but not a 机.

**Action-availability of a 机**. For an agent-focus $a \in A$:

$$
\text{可行之机}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{机}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \exists\, i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma):\; \text{行}(a, \Gamma, i) \text{ is realizable}
$$

> 机 = 场间分歧之处，择一间则开闭异路。

### 合义

势为累积梯度，机为分歧门。**于机失行，则势自决之；于机识间而行，乃义之初显**。义之论待二十四节。

## 九、聚散

### 间相

聚者，势之凝；散者，形之解。聚非凭空成物，散非彻底归无。形散而迹存，迹入后续而成积。然若不变量俱断，所开之路俱闭，则**死与坍缩真实**——非生之另一相，乃间之真绝。

> **聚散者，势与形之往复也。死者，聚散之极端，亦真有。**

### 物相

**Gathering and dispersal are the formation and dissolution of stable predicate-truths along an orbit. Real collapse is the special case of dispersal in which no continuation predicate remains supported.**

An object gathers when a predicate $P$ becomes stably true under the field's dynamics. It disperses when this stability is lost. Dispersal need not be annihilation: residues, constraints, memories, and trace-affordances may seed later formations. But some dispersals are real collapse — the predicate fails *and* no successor predicate inherits its constraints into a continuable structure.

### 场相

Let $P : \mathfrak{F} \to \{0, 1\}$ be a predicate over field states. Let $B_s(\Gamma)$ denote the scale-$s$ orbit neighborhood of $\Gamma$.

**Form / 聚 (gathering).**

$$
\text{形}(P, \Gamma, s) \;\iff\; P(\Gamma) = 1 \;\wedge\; \forall\, \Gamma' \in B_s(\Gamma):\; P(\Gamma') = 1
$$

**Dispersal / 散.**

$$
\text{散}(P, \Gamma_n) \;\iff\; \exists\, m > n,\;\; \forall\, k \geq m:\; P(\Gamma_k) = 0
$$

**Trace transmission.** Let $\text{不变}(P)$ denote the structural invariants of $P$ (parameters, constraints, attractor signature). Then transformation between predicates:

$$
\text{化}(P \to Q) \;\iff\; \text{不变}(P) \subseteq \text{条件}(Q) \;\vee\; \text{不变}(P) \text{ enters } \mathcal{C}(\Gamma_{>m})
$$

**Real collapse / 真坍缩.**

$$
\boxed{\;\text{真坍}(P, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{散}(P, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \neg\, \exists\, Q:\; \text{化}(P \to Q)\;}
$$

— predicate dies *and* its invariants enter no successor.

> 聚 = 场谓之暂稳；散 = 场谓之解稳；真坍缩 = 解稳无续。

### 合义

聚散非二事，乃同一场续中稳定与解稳之交替。**死与坍缩为真——此论不诡辩生灭一相而忽视真绝**。能否于他场显其迹，乃聚散之分水。

# 卷三 · 判准

## 十、开闭

### 间相

开者，间不尽也；闭者，未尽绝也。开非更多，闭非更少。开是**未未闭**，闭是**未已绝**。一极简之事可开，一极繁之事可闭。繁简非标，**能否续、应、转、修**乃其标。

> **开者，未仍可生；闭者，未不复生。**

### 物相

**Openness is the field's capacity to generate, respond, revise, and regenerate valid intervals. Closure is the absence of this capacity.**

Openness is not the cardinality of options. A state may have many available intervals yet fail to remain responsive to environment, fail to revise its own constraints, or fail to regenerate intervals after disturbance. Conversely, a sparse state may remain genuinely open if its scant intervals retain these four capacities. Openness is the *living* capacity for valid continuation, not the *enumerated* size of an option set.

### 场相

**Continuability** decomposes into four primitive capacities:

$$
\text{可续}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \neq \emptyset \;\wedge\; \text{应}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{修}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{复生}_F(\Gamma)
$$

where
- $\text{应}_F$ — field can respond to perturbations from $\mathcal{C}$ or $R$
- $\text{修}_F$ — field can revise its own constraints (closed-loop on $\mathcal{C}$)
- $\text{复生}_F$ — field can regenerate $\text{间}_F$ after partial collapse

**Definition (开).**

$$
\text{开}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \exists\, i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma):\; \text{可续}_F(\Gamma \oplus_F i)
$$

**The four-aspect decomposition / 场开四相** (canonical refinement):

$$
\text{开}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{未}_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{可}_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{应}_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{转}_F(\Gamma)
$$

| 中文 | 英文 | 形式 |
|---|---|---|
| 未 (Wei) | unformed-domain remains | $\exists$ unactualized region in $\mathcal{M}$ relevant to $\Gamma$ |
| 可 (Ke) | becoming remains possible | $\text{间}_F(\Gamma) \neq \emptyset$ |
| 应 (Ying) | response remains possible | $\text{应}_F(\Gamma)$ |
| 转 (Zhuan) | revision remains possible | $\text{修}_F(\Gamma) \wedge \text{复生}_F(\Gamma)$ |

**Closure** is the negation:

$$
\text{闭}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \neg\, \text{开}_F(\Gamma)
$$

> 开 = 场可生、可应、可转、可修；闭 = 此能力丧失。

### 合义

开闭为全论之**判准**。其余概念——中、和、善、仁、义、礼、智——皆为开闭在不同维度之具体显。**离开闭，全论无尺**。

## 十一、伪开三型

闭有二义：**成闭** (bad closure) 与**护闭** (protective closure)。成闭者，令未尽之域不复开；护闭者，暂闭一处以护更大之开。**护闭非开之缺，乃开之保**——膜护生命，规护信任，拒护尊严。

更难者，**伪开** (pseudo-openness)——外示为开，内成其闭；以一时之可，夺长远之未。

### 形式定义

**成闭**：

$$
\text{成闭}_F(\Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \text{闭}_F(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \;\wedge\; \forall\, \text{wider scope } \Gamma^\sharp \supseteq \Gamma:\; \neg\, \text{开}_F(\Gamma^\sharp \oplus_F i)
$$

**护闭** (with respect to a wider scope $\Gamma^\sharp$ and local pathway $p$)：

$$
\text{护闭}_F(\Gamma, i, p, \Gamma^\sharp) \;\iff\; \text{闭}(i, p) \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma^\sharp \oplus_F i) \;\wedge\; \neg\, \text{开}_F(\Gamma^\sharp \oplus_F \neg i)
$$

— $i$ closes $p$, but the wider scope is open *with* $i$ and would not be open without it.

**捕获 (capture)**：

$$
\text{夺}_F(\Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \exists\, b \in A:\; \text{开}_b(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \nearrow \;\wedge\; \forall\, a \neq b:\; \text{开}_a(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \searrow
$$

— one focus's openness grows by absorbing others'.

**元闭 (meta-closure)**：

$$
\text{元闭}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{the rules generating } \text{间}_F \text{ are themselves frozen}
$$

— at the level of *which intervals can become valid*, the field has lost its generative capacity. (Formal counterpart in §13.)

**伪开**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{伪开}_F(\Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \exists\, k \in A:\; \text{似开}_k(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \;\wedge\; \big[\text{成闭}_F \,\vee\, \text{夺}_F \,\vee\, \text{元闭}_F\big](\Gamma \oplus_F i)\;}
$$

— *appears* open from focus $k$'s perspective, but actually leads to one of the three closure modes.

### 三型分解

| 型 | 中文 | 英文 |
|---|---|---|
| I | 局部开，整体闭 | Local-open / global-close |
| II | 短期开，长时闭 | Short-horizon-open / long-horizon-close |
| III | 表层开，元间闭 | Surface-open / meta-close |

> **成闭为恶；护闭为中；伪开为闭之诈。**

此节为鲁棒性之关键。若无护闭，则开滑向放任；若无伪开，则善易被表象欺骗；若无尺度，则局部开可误作全局善。

## 十二、自相似

间生过程之展开，**无中心之尺度，无主从之等级**。每一尺度，皆有其聚焦、其相续、其条贯、其对偶维度，皆需中、和、美、德之具体显。同一原则——即护开——**于每尺度具体显现，而形式同构**。是为**自相似**。

### 形式定义

**自相似 (formal scale-invariance of openness criteria)**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{自相似}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, s_1, s_2 \in \mathcal{S}:\; \text{开}_F^{s_1}(\cdot) \,\overset{\text{form}}{\equiv}\, \text{开}_F^{s_2}(\cdot)\;}
$$

i.e. across any two scales $s_1, s_2$ in the scale lattice $\mathcal{S}$, the openness predicates have the same logical form — only the focus $A^s$, interval set $\text{间}_F^s$, and constraint set $\mathcal{C}^s$ differ in their instantiation.

**Functorial reading.** Let $\mathfrak{F}^s$ denote the field-state space at scale $s$. The openness criterion is a natural transformation:

$$
\text{开}_F : \mathfrak{F}^{(\cdot)} \;\Rightarrow\; \{0,1\}^{(\cdot)}
$$

i.e. $\text{开}_F$ commutes with scale-coarsening / refinement up to the relevant projection.

### 用途

每一尺度之节，皆问同一形式之事：**此境之行此构，护开乎？成闭乎？是否极于一端？是否致和？是否伪开而实闭？是否当护闭以护更大之开？**

故"细胞之于身、个人之于群、企业之于市场、文明之于地球"——非比喻，乃**同形之节**。形式同则推理同；境性异则具体异。

> **形同而境异：是以一论可通诸尺度而不僭尺度。**

自相似非言"尺度无差"——恰相反，自相似立**形式之同**，方使尺度之差**有可比之轴**。无形式同，则尺度之异即不可比；尺度之异即不可比，则"癌于细胞似续，于身则闭"无意义。**自相似与尺度差，互为支柱**。

## 十三、因生因

间生过程之深节：**积非但为过程之产物，积复能生积；因不止生果，因生因之新方式**。

### 形式定义

**维度自开 (dimensional self-opening)**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{开维}\big(\Gamma_t \to \Gamma_{t+n}\big) \;\iff\; \exists\, i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma_{t+n}):\; \text{合法}_F(i, \cdot)\big|_{\Gamma_t} \text{ is undefined}\;}
$$

谓：**新可然之开，非于已有可然空间增一新点，乃令可然空间本身扩张至新维度**——存在一间，其合法性之判据于 $t$ 时根本无定义，因 $t$ 时之结构不足以承载该判据。

**生成规则之演化 (evolution of validity rules)**：

$$
\text{合法}_F\big(\cdot, \Gamma_{t+n}\big) \;\not\equiv\; \text{合法}_F\big(\cdot, \Gamma_t\big) \quad \text{at the rule level, not just the value level}
$$

观此扩张之具体相：

$$
\begin{array}{rcl}
\text{物质既出} & \implies & \text{「基于物质之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{化学既起} & \implies & \text{「基于化学反应之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{生命既出} & \implies & \text{「基于自我复制之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{神经既出} & \implies & \text{「基于神经之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{语言既出} & \implies & \text{「基于符号之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{文明既出} & \implies & \text{「基于反思之过程」此一维度方有} \\
\text{人造心智既出} & \implies & \text{「基于人造心智之过程」此一维度方有}
\end{array}
$$

每一维度之开辟，皆使原本不存在之过程类型成为可能。**因创因，果创果，因果创因果之因果**。

### 与 MetaClosure 之对偶

第十一节之 $\text{元闭}$ 即"未来 $\text{间}_F$ 生成规则之能力被锁死"；故：

$$
\text{开维}(\Gamma_t \to \Gamma_{t+n}) \;\;\overset{\text{对偶}}{\longleftrightarrow}\;\; \neg\, \text{元闭}_F\big(\Gamma_{[t, t+n]}\big)
$$

因生因即过程之**自我加速**——非外力推之，亦非目的引之，乃**积之自递归与因果之自递归**。

**与格物致知之对偶 (§16)**: 格物致知是范式内之合法导出（守史中已立之形/因/结）；因生因是范式之自我开维（破史中未立之新形/因/结）。**两者合，方为合法之全机**：

$$
\text{合法}_F(i, \Gamma_t) \iff \big[\text{格物致知}(i \mid \mathcal{H}_t)\big] \,\vee\, \big[\text{开维}(i, \mathcal{H}_t \to \Gamma_t)\big]
$$

无格物则合法无据（凭空立法），无因生因则合法不能新（拘古不前）。**两者共撑论之 epistemology**。

> **因生因者，间之自我开维也。无此节，"间生"之"生"少其半——只解释续，不解释涌现新可然。**

## 十四、演化与尺度

道非外在目的，乃过程在场中能续、能应、能转、能共开之**活条贯**。演化即过程在尺度中之筛选：

> **演化筛选能在某场、某尺度、某时间窗中继续者。**

### 形式定义

**续之能 (continuation capacity)**：

$$
\text{续能}\big(p, \Gamma, s, t\big) \;:=\; \mathbb{P}\Big[\, p \text{ persists in } \text{轨}_F(\Gamma) \;\big|\; \text{scale } s,\; \text{horizon } t \,\Big]
$$

**筛选 (selection)**：

$$
\mathbb{P}_{\sigma_F}\big(p \,\big|\, \Gamma, s, t\big) \;\;\propto\;\; \text{续能}(p, \Gamma, s, t)
$$

**在道上 (OnDao)**：

$$
\text{在道}(p, \Gamma, s, t) \;\iff\; \text{续能}(p, \Gamma, s, t) \;\geq\; \theta_{\text{persist}}
$$

— $p$'s continuation capacity at scale $s$ over horizon $t$ is sufficient for persistence, replication, repair, or transformation. $\theta_{\text{persist}}$ is境性.

### 尺度警告

**OnDao is not transitive across scales**:

$$
\text{在道}(p, \Gamma, s_{\text{local}}, t_{\text{short}}) \;\;\not\!\!\implies\;\; \text{在道}(p, \Gamma, s_{\text{global}}, t_{\text{long}})
$$

局部之胜，可为整体之闭。短时之开，可为长时之闭。节点之扩张，可为共场之坍缩。

| 例 | 局部 (small $s$, short $t$) | 全局 (large $s$, long $t$) |
|---|---|---|
| Cancer cells | OnDao at replication | OffDao at organism |
| Molochian competition | OnDao for single actor | OffDao for civilization |
| Metric optimization | OnDao within metric-space | OffDao within full value-field |

中文曰：

> 癌之于细胞，似续；于身，则闭。  
> 争之于一己，似胜；于共场，则陷。  
> 指标之于机器，似明；于万维生命，则蔽。

**真在道上 (真道)**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{真道}(p, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, (s, t) \in \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{T}_{\text{rel}}: \;\; \text{在道}(p, \Gamma, s, t) \;\wedge\; \neg\, \big[\text{成闭}_F \,\vee\, \text{夺}_F \,\vee\, \text{伪开}_F \,\vee\, \text{元闭}_F\big]\;}
$$

智者之事，非盲从演化，乃**辨演化之筛在何尺度上护开、在何尺度上成闭**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{正筛}_F(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{selection pressures in } \Gamma \text{ are shaped such that } \sigma_F \text{ favors } \text{共开}_F \text{ across wider } s \text{ and longer } t\;}
$$

> **治道者，非逆演化，乃正其筛；使所筛者不止于一己一时之续，而能合诸开之共续。**

制度、礼、教育、协议、文化、市场、法律、算法、alignment——皆可如此理解：

> **They reshape the selection field. They change which patterns can survive, spread, stabilize, and become natural.**

古言曰：

> **圣人不强万物以从己，乃正其场，使善者易生，伪开难久，成闭难成。**

# 卷四 · 境与模

## 十五、境

境者，**诸间相交之活整体**。无离境自立之物。物乃境中暂稳之聚焦；心乃境中自感之聚焦；事乃境中诸间相遇之显。

$$
\text{物}(a, \Gamma, s) \;\iff\; \text{稳焦}(a, \Gamma, s) \quad\text{(scale-relative)}
$$

$$
I(a, \Gamma) \;:=\; \text{投}_a\big(\text{间}_F(\Gamma)\big) \;\;\text{— the affordance profile of } a \text{ at } \Gamma
$$

> **境非物之集合，物乃境之聚焦。**

境是三相之共同母体：事在境中生，物在境中成，场即境之形式化。气无离境之行，间无离境之生。

## 十六、格物致知

### 间相

合法之间，非凭空而立；其值由**史**而生。

每场之续，留迹于积；积之相续而成史；史中可识其形，可究其因，可辨其构——验之以**形式**、**因果**、**结构** 三者，则得后场之合法之间。

> **格物致知者，由史提炼合法之方也。**

王阳明曰知行合一——知非外在所予，知由格物中生；行由知出，复入于积，再为下次格物之资。**知行合一者，史与合法之相生也**。

故 §二 立 $\text{合法}_F$ 时所言 "境性，本文不予指定"，非论之未尽，乃留此节以言其方：合法之具体内容因境而异，但**生此内容之方一也，即格物致知**。

### 物相

**The validity predicate $\text{合法}_F$ — left境性 in §2 — receives its concrete content through 格物致知: the operation of extracting validity criteria from accumulated history. The extraction operates in three modes: formal, causal, and structural verification.**

This grounds an epistemological claim that previously was placed beyond the work's specification: how does one *know* what counts as a valid interval? Not by external decree, not by prior intuition, not by closed axiomatization. **By extracting from 史**.

The extraction is iterative. Each new $\Gamma_t$ contributes to the history $\mathcal{H}_{t+1}$, from which $\text{合法}_F(\cdot, \Gamma_{t+1})$ is in turn refined. **This is 知行合一 in formal dress**: action enters history; history shapes validity; validity constrains the next action; the next action enters history again. The cycle is constitutive, not a regrettable limitation.

### 场相

**Definition (史 / accumulated history)**. Let $\mathcal{H}_t$ denote the orbit up to but not including time $t$:

$$
\mathcal{H}_t \;:=\; \text{轨}_F^{(t-1)}(\Gamma_0) \;=\; (\Gamma_0, \Gamma_1, \ldots, \Gamma_{t-1})
$$

Note: 史 is the temporal-trajectory reading of 积 (§26); 积 is the structural-substrate reading of the same content. They are dual presentations.

**Definition (格物致知 / extraction operator)**. The validity predicate at $\Gamma_t$ is determined by three-mode verification against history:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{合法}_F(i, \Gamma_t) \;\iff\; \Phi_\text{形}(i \mid \mathcal{H}_t) \;\wedge\; \Phi_\text{因}(i \mid \mathcal{H}_t) \;\wedge\; \Phi_\text{结}(i \mid \mathcal{H}_t)\;}
$$

where the three verification modes are:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Phi_\text{形}(i \mid \mathcal{H}) &:\;\; i \text{ 实例化某形式 pattern, 该 pattern 已在 } \mathcal{H} \text{ 中显} \\
\Phi_\text{因}(i \mid \mathcal{H}) &:\;\; i \text{ 之 causal precedents 在 } \mathcal{H} \text{ 中可考} \\
\Phi_\text{结}(i \mid \mathcal{H}) &:\;\; i \text{ 不破 } \mathcal{H} \text{ 之结构 invariants}
\end{aligned}
$$

**与因生因之对偶 (§13)**. 格物致知是范式内之合法导出；**因生因**是范式之自我开维——存在 $i$，其 $\Phi$ 之条件于 $\mathcal{H}_t$ 中根本无定义。两者合：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{合法}_F(i, \Gamma_t) \;\iff\; \big[\,\text{格物致知}(i \mid \mathcal{H}_t)\,\big] \;\vee\; \big[\,\text{开维}(i, \mathcal{H}_t \to \Gamma_t)\,\big]\;}
$$

—— 合法或源于 history's lessons, 或源于 history's rules themselves evolving。两者皆生合法；前者守范式，后者破范式。

**知行合一之 cycle**:

$$
\Gamma_t \;\xrightarrow{\;\text{行}\;}\; \Gamma_{t+1} \;\xrightarrow{\;\text{入积}\;}\; \mathcal{H}_{t+1} \;\xrightarrow{\;\text{格物}\;}\; \text{合法}_F(\cdot, \Gamma_{t+1}) \;\xrightarrow{\;\text{识}\;}\; \text{下次行}
$$

知 由 行 而生（through interaction with history）；行 由 知 而正（through validity-knowledge）。**知行合一非二事，乃此 cycle 之自闭环**。

**与礼之承**. 礼 (§34) 即 格物致知 在多代尺度之结晶——经多代受校而稳定的 selection-shaping 工具，其内容是**多代之 history's lessons 之沉淀**。法律、协议、文化、教育，皆此理之诸形。礼之死，乃 cycle 断——cycle 一断，合法不再由格物而生，乃由 ossified form 而僵。

**与四校之承**. 模与四校 (§17) 即 格物致知 之 quality control: $\text{回响}_F$ (场之回响) 验 $\Phi_\text{因}$ 之实在；$\text{众应}$ (众聚焦之回应) 验 $\Phi_\text{结}$ 之共识；$\text{后果迹}$ 验 $\Phi_\text{形}$ 之延续；$\text{互证}_F$ (诸模互证) 验三模之一致。**四校是格物致知之自我校正机制**。

### 合义

事见史出合法，物见 affordance 由经验而立，场见 $\text{合法}_F$ 由 $\mathcal{H}_t$ 之三模验证。**格物致知是论之 epistemic engine**——它把 §2 之 "境性，不予指定" 转为 "由史而生"。

且大学之"格物致知"、王阳明之"知行合一"，于此立形式之骨：知非先在，知由格物而生；行非外在，行由知出复入积。**论与中国古典认识论之骨脉，于此节明立**。

## 十七、模与四校

模者，**识境之一指**——非境自身。模是境中一聚焦以一尺度、一关切、一方法，取境之一面而成之表征。**模不可绝对，然模有高下**：能显理、识机、辨伪、护开、导行者为善模。

### 形式定义

**模 (model)**：

$$
M_k(\Gamma) \;:=\; \text{投}\Big(\Gamma \;\Big|\; \text{聚焦}_k,\; \text{尺度}_k,\; \text{关切}_k,\; \text{法}_k\Big)
$$

**模之高下**：

$$
M_1 \succeq_\Gamma M_2 \;\iff\; M_1 \text{ supports Open}_F\text{-preserving action on } \Gamma \text{ at least as well as } M_2 \text{ across relevant } (s, t)
$$

**模闭 (model closure)**：

$$
\text{模闭}(M, \Gamma) \;\iff\; M \text{ treated as exhaustive} \;\wedge\; \text{this treatment reduces future } \text{间}_F\text{-recognition}
$$

### 四校 (four-fold calibration)

感可偏，情可蔽，积可成习。故模与感皆须四校：

$$
\text{四校}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{回响}_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{众应}(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{后果迹}(\Gamma \to \Gamma') \,\wedge\, \text{互证}_F(\Gamma)
$$

| 校 | 中文 | 英文 |
|---|---|---|
| $\text{回响}_F$ | 场之回响 | field's reflexive response |
| $\text{众应}$ | 众聚焦之回应 | other foci's responses |
| $\text{后果迹}$ | 后果之轨迹 | downstream consequence trace $\Gamma \to \Gamma'$ |
| $\text{互证}_F$ | 诸模互证 | cross-model consistency check |

> **模者，指境之器；四校者，防一己之感自称为道。**

四校不保证收敛于真——保证之事需聚焦在境中之实际行与受校之诚。**四校能助显伪、显未尽，不能代行**。形式系统不能为机处选行；模不能代境而立。

# 卷五 · 价值之相

中、和、美、德四节皆三相全显。此四者为开闭在不同维度之具体显，是**价值之根**——非外加规范，乃护开之自然之态。

## 十八、中

### 间相

中者，**不使开闭于一端**也。中非中点，非折衷，非平均。中者，识一境之对偶张力，使两端之开皆不被一端吞没。必要时，中亦能护闭一处，以护更大之开。

> **中者，间不偏闭也。**

### 物相

**中 is the dynamic field-state in which a polarity's two poles each retain live continuation; neither has captured the other.**

A field in 中 is not statistically centered between two extremes — that is a category error. *Zhong* is a structural property of polarity-openness: the field's continuation capacity remains live along *both* polar dimensions, even when activation is currently asymmetric. *Zhong* admits unilateral protective closure when one pole's wider openness depends on the other being temporarily restrained.

### 场相

For a polarity $\mathfrak{d} = (u, v)$ on $\Gamma$:

**Pole-extremity**:

$$
\text{极}_u(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \big\| \nabla w_\Gamma \big\|_u \gg \big\| \nabla w_\Gamma \big\|_v \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{开}^v_F(\Gamma)
$$

**Definition (中)**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{中}_F(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \neg\,\text{极}_u(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{极}_v(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \,\wedge\, \text{开}^u_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{开}^v_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

equivalently:

$$
\text{中}_F(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \forall\, x \in \{u, v\}:\; \text{开}^x_F(\Gamma) \;\text{is not closed beyond repair by activation at } x^c
$$

> 中 = 场中对偶不被一端吞没之动态。

### 合义

事见其不极，物见其多 affordance 可活，场见其 polarity-openness 得保。**中是开在对偶中之形式**。

## 十九、平

### 间相

平者，**场无扰动而仍开**也。

势小而场不躁，气定而隙未绝。平非死寂——死寂者，无扰动而开尽闭；平非僵静——僵静者，似无变而生意已断。**平者，活之未发，未必发之活**。

平为众动之底——一切扰动皆自平起，亦终归于平。然平之为平，不在无扰动，在**有扰动而能复归**。

> **平者，势近零而开未失也。**

### 物相

**平 is the field-state of vanishing gradient — minimal perturbation in the activation landscape — coupled with sustained openness. It is not stillness as death; it is rest as readiness.**

Without the openness condition, low gradient signals collapse: a field with $\nabla w \to 0$ and $\neg\,\text{开}_F$ is a closed field, dead matter, terminal absorption. 平 is the conjunction — calm *and* alive. From the object-side, 平 is a system at low excitation but with intact response capacity: a poised configuration whose disturbance would generate response, repair, and revision.

平 is the floor of the good region: the lowest sustainable perturbation that is still alive.

### 场相

**Definition (平)** ——

$$
\boxed{\;\text{平}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \big\| \text{势}_\Gamma \big\| \;<\; \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

i.e. the magnitude of the activation gradient is below the calm threshold, *and* the openness predicate holds.

**Distinction from closure.** With $\theta_{\text{calm}}$ as the calm threshold:

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \text{开}_F(\Gamma) &\Longrightarrow\; \text{平}(\Gamma) \quad \text{(calm-alive)} \\[3pt]
\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{开}_F(\Gamma) &\Longrightarrow\; \text{闭}_F(\Gamma) \quad \text{(calm-dead)}
\end{array}
$$

低梯度本身不分善恶；分善恶者，是否仍能开。

**Recoverability** (one direction of 平's liveness):

$$
\text{平}(\Gamma) \;\implies\; \exists\, \delta\text{-perturbation: } \Gamma \rightsquigarrow \Gamma',\;\; \big\|\text{势}_{\Gamma'}\big\| > \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma')
$$

— an open-calm field can be perturbed back into active openness, i.e. into 和 (next section).

> 平 = 梯度近零之活态。

### 合义

事见无扰之续，物见 poised configuration，场见 $\nabla w \to 0$ 而 $\text{开}_F$ 不失。**平是开在静中之形式**。

平、和为对偶之二极，**皆在好之区间内**——下节即立此区间。

## 二十、和

### 间相

和者，**诸开相通而不相吞**也。和非同——同者以一吞多；和非斗——斗者以己闭彼；和非隔——隔者各守其闭。和者，差异仍在，而诸开相通，互生而不相夺。

> **和者，各开而互通也。**

### 物相

**和 is the relational composition of multiple foci such that each retains its openness, the openness profiles are mutually compatible, and there is non-zero relational coupling between them.**

*He* is not sameness (which is uniformity by absorption) and not mere coexistence (which can be openness-without-relation, i.e. isolation). *He* requires three things simultaneously: each focus open, their open-profiles compatible, and live coupling among them. Difference must remain — but as participation, not as wall.

### 场相

For a focus subset $B \subseteq A$ on $\Gamma$:

**Co-openness**:

$$
\text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \big(\forall\, a \in B:\; \text{开}_a(\Gamma)\big) \,\wedge\, \text{相合}\big(\{\text{开}_a\}_{a \in B}, \Gamma\big) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{夺}(B, \Gamma)
$$

**Relational flow**:

$$
\text{流}(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \exists\, a \neq b \in B:\; \kappa(\mathcal{I}_a, \mathcal{I}_b) \neq 0 \;\wedge\; \text{the coupling is not capture-typed}
$$

**Definition (和)**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{和}_F(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \big\| \text{势}_\Gamma \big\| \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{流}(B, \Gamma)\;}
$$

i.e. perturbation present (gradient above the calm threshold), but distributed across foci such that openness is jointly preserved and relational coupling flows non-trivially.

> 和 = 有扰而共开，且诸开互通。

**Polarity with 平.** 平 and 和 are the two sustainable poles of the good region:

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\text{平}(\Gamma) & : \;\; \big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma) \\[3pt]
\text{和}_F(B, \Gamma) & : \;\; \big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{流}(B, \Gamma)
\end{array}
$$

二者皆在好之内，分于扰动之有无。

### 合义

中治对偶之极，平治静而活，和治动而共。**中、平、和三者，开在不同维度之三显**。下卷立 [平, 和] 区间为好。

## 二十一、美

### 间相

美者，**心遇开而自开**也。心遇一物、一景、一声、一人，若其间被引动，其未被唤醒，其感应开一新维，则谓之美。美非利，非快，非正——**美者，开感也**。

> **美者，开与开相遇而互显也。**

### 物相

**Beauty is the encounter-event between a sensing focus and an object-focus in which the sensing focus's openness profile is enlarged, without inducing closure elsewhere in the field.**

Beauty is not utility (which subordinates the object to a need), not pleasure (which can be a closed loop), and not normative correctness (which can be empty form). It is the disclosure of latent affordance-depth in the encountered, registered as an enlargement of openness in the encountering. Deep beauty further increases the *relational* openness between the two — not merely the perceiver's private state.

### 场相

For a sensing focus $m$ encountering an object focus $o$ at field state $\Gamma$:

**Beauty**:

$$
\text{美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{遇}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\implies\; \Big[\text{开}_m(\Gamma_+) > \text{开}_m(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{成闭}_F(\Gamma_+)\Big]
$$

where $\Gamma_+ = \Gamma \oplus_F i_{\text{enc}}$ is the post-encounter field.

**Deep beauty** (relational enlargement):

$$
\text{深美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{关开}(m, o, \Gamma_+) > \text{关开}(m, o, \Gamma)
$$

> 美 = 相遇而开，且不闭其场；深美 = 关系亦开。

### 合义

事见心开，物见 affordance depth，场见 encounter 改变 openness profile。**美是开在感应中之形式**。

## 二十二、德

### 间相

德者，**护开之积**也。一行合中，不足谓德；久行而积，遇境自能感其机、守其中、致其和、辨其伪开、用其护闭，乃谓德。

> **德者，护开久而成性也。**

### 物相

**德 is a focus's stable disposition toward open-preserving action, distributed across a wide range of contexts, and remaining responsive rather than rigid.**

Virtue is not an abstract rule, not an inner essence, and not a memorized behavior. It is the orbit-level signature of a focus whose interval-selection consistently preserves openness across diverse $\Gamma$. Living virtue is calibratable: it updates under the four-fold check rather than ossifying into form. Rigidity is closure-disguised-as-virtue.

### 场相

For an agent-focus $a$:

**Virtue**:

$$
\text{德}(a) \;\iff\; \forall\, \Gamma \in \mathfrak{F}_{\text{rel}}:\; \mathbb{P}\Big[\, \text{行}(a, \Gamma, i) \text{ preserves Open}_F \,\Big] \;\geq\; \theta_{\text{virtue}}
$$

**True virtue** (calibratable & non-rigid):

$$
\text{真德}(a) \;\iff\; \text{德}(a) \;\wedge\; \text{四校}_F(a, \cdot) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{僵}(a)
$$

> 德 = 场中反复护开之行，沉淀为稳定回应；真德兼受四校。

### 合义

事见行久，物见 disposition，场见 tendency 被稳定化。**德是开在时间积累中之形式**。中、和、美、德四者互显——和必含美，美必含中，德必含中而显于和与美之能。

# 卷六 · 心物

理、心、情、积四节为间生过程之相互显发。**非四物，乃同一过程之四相**——理为条贯之相，心为聚焦之相，情为关系之相，积为沉淀之相。此卷以一相为主、他相略带，不再三相全显。

## 二十三、理

理者，**过程自显之条贯**。理不在事先（未有事时无可识之条贯），不在事中（不附属于某具体事而显于事之相续），不在事外（无外于过程之处所容理）。**理即事之自显为条贯之事自身**。

$$
\text{理}(\Gamma) \;:=\; \text{条贯}\Big(\text{轨}_F(\Gamma),\; \text{尺度},\; \text{聚焦}\Big)
$$

For an object focus $a$:

$$
\text{理}(a, \Gamma) \;:=\; \text{coherent regularity of } \big(\sigma_t(a),\, R_t,\, \mathcal{C}_t\big) \text{ across orbit evolution}
$$

> **理者，气行而有纹。生生之纹也。**

故问"理之根据何在"已误——此问预设理为有据之物。理无根据，以其本非有据之物；**理之为理，在过程自身能自显为可识之条贯**。

## 二十四、心

心者，**过程自感之一聚焦**。心非过程外之主，亦非孤立之我。心者，能感、能应、能识间、能受校正之处。

$$
\text{主}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{感}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{择}(a, \text{间}_F(\Gamma)) \,\wedge\, \text{行}(a, \Gamma, \cdot) \,\wedge\, \text{四校}_F(a, \Gamma)
$$

$$
\text{心}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{主}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{建模}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{反馈合}(a, \Gamma)
$$

> **心者，场中能感间者也；气能自感其间也。**

一身之心、一群之心、一物种之心、一文明之心，皆此聚焦之不同尺度。心非本体——心是体之一显，体亦无独立可言。**心与体互显，非二物**。

## 二十五、情

情者，**心于关系中之自显**。无关系则情无所生；无心则情无所感。情非心之外物，亦非关系之外物，乃心与关系相遇而显之态。

For relation $r = (a, b) \in R$:

$$
\text{情}(a, b, \Gamma) \;:=\; \text{情态}\Big(\rho(r),\, \sigma(a),\, \sigma(b),\, \text{史}(r),\, \mathcal{C}\Big)
$$

It modulates future weights of intervals involving $a, b, r$:

$$
w_\Gamma\big(i\big)\big|_{i \ni \{a, b, r\}} \;\;\longmapsto\;\; w_\Gamma(i) \cdot \phi\big(\text{情}(a, b, \Gamma)\big)
$$

> **情者，关系入心而动；气入关系而有色。**

情之得正，即不闭之情。情可极之——极爱、极怒、极忧——皆收情于一端而失对偶之张力。情之得正者，非无情，亦非节情，**乃情之保持其开**。

## 二十六、积

积者，**过程之沉淀**。凡已行之事，未必存其形，然其迹入后续，反限未来之间。积可成德，亦可成执；可护开，亦可闭开。

$$
\mathcal{A}_t \;:=\; \text{迹}\big(\Gamma_0, \ldots, \Gamma_t\big), \quad \mathcal{C}_{t+1} = \text{更新}\big(\mathcal{C}_t, \mathcal{A}_t\big), \quad \text{间}_F(\Gamma_{t+n}) \text{ depends on } \mathcal{A}_t
$$

**Living vs dead accumulation**:

$$
\text{活积}(\mathcal{A}) \;\iff\; \text{积}(\mathcal{A}) \,\wedge\, \text{可修}(\mathcal{A}) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{MetaClosure-inducing}
$$

$$
\text{死积}(\mathcal{A}) \;\iff\; \text{积}(\mathcal{A}) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{可修}(\mathcal{A}) \,\wedge\, \text{MetaClosure-inducing}
$$

> **积者，往者入今而限未来。**

塑造之积者护开（德是其极），锁定之积者成闭（执是其极）。

# 卷七 · 道之相

道非外在目的，乃过程在场中能续、能应、能转、能共开之活条贯。本卷由善恶之分入，立**好之区间**[平, 和]，由此得**自由**与**繁荣**——此皆道在不同尺度之具体显。

## 二十七、善恶

### 间相

善者，**护开、续开、共开**者也。  
恶者，**成闭、夺开、伪开而实闭**者也。  
善不在最大化某物，恶不在减少某量。**善在未不被吞，诸开能共在，后来仍可修、可应、可转**。

> **善护未，恶绝未。**

### 物相

**Good action preserves, restores, or deepens the field's open-continuation across the relevant scope, scale, and time, without inducing pseudo-openness, capture, or meta-closure. Evil is the negation in any of these modes.**

Good is not the maximization of any quantity — whether utility, preference, welfare, or virtue itself. Maximization of any single quantity, taken to its limit, induces capture or meta-closure on other dimensions. The criterion is structural, not quantitative: does the action keep the field's *capacity to continue, repair, respond, and transform* alive across the right scales?

The next section (§27) gives this criterion an inner topography by locating *good* as the gradient-interval $[\text{平}, \text{和}]$.

### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{善}_F(a, \Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \text{行}(a, \Gamma, i) \text{ preserves } \text{开}_F \text{ across } (s, t) \text{ relevant} \;\wedge\; \neg\,\big[\text{伪开}_F \,\vee\, \text{夺}_F \,\vee\, \text{元闭}_F\big]\;}
$$

$$
\text{恶}_F(a, \Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \text{成闭}_F(\Gamma') \,\vee\, \text{夺}_F(\Gamma') \,\vee\, \text{伪开}_F(\Gamma, i) \,\vee\, \text{元闭}_F(\Gamma')
$$

其中 $\Gamma' = \Gamma \oplus_F i$。

> 善 = 护共开；恶 = 成闭、夺开、伪开。

### 合义

善恶非约定，非利益之代名，非相对之偏好。**善恶有形式判准——开闭与其三型伪诈**。然其判须受四校与尺度校正——故善之识仍须工夫，非可一次定之。

## 二十八、好与不好

### 间相

好者，**势在平和之间**也。

平为静中之活，和为动中之共。势小而开，曰平；势大而共开，曰和。**自平至和，皆在好之内**。势小而闭，是死；势大而夺，是暴。**好之外，皆不好**。

> **好者，势在 [平, 和] 之内也；不好者，出此区间，或落于死，或入于暴。**

故好非一点，乃一区间；非平之极，亦非和之极，乃**平和之间之全段**。我之意图当落此段——或近平以休，或近和以行；皆道之内。

### 物相

**Good is not a point but an interval on the gradient-magnitude axis, bounded below by 平 (vanishing perturbation, openness preserved) and above by 和 (active perturbation, co-openness preserved). Not-good is the symmetric exterior — closure-by-stagnation below 平, capture-by-violence above 和.**

The intuition: good admits both rest and action. A field can be good while quiet (preparing, waiting, recovering, gestating) or good while active (engaging, harmonizing, transforming together). What is excluded on the calm side is death; what is excluded on the active side is capture. The interval is the *open-preserving* range of perturbation magnitudes, with appropriate distribution.

This reframing has a sharp consequence: *the polarity within the good is not a polarity between good and bad*. 平 and 和 are both good — choosing between them is wisdom, not morality.

### 场相

**Definition (好).** Let $\Gamma$ be a field state with focus subset $B \subseteq A$. Then:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{好}(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \text{平}(\Gamma) \;\vee\; \text{和}_F(B, \Gamma)\;}
$$

equivalently, as a connected region on the perturbation axis subject to openness constraints:

$$
\text{好}(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \exists\, \mu \in \big[\,0,\, \mu_{\max}(B, \Gamma)\,\big]:\;\; \big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| = \mu \;\wedge\; \Phi_\mu(\Gamma, B)
$$

where $\mu_{\max}(B, \Gamma)$ is the maximum perturbation magnitude consistent with $\text{共开}(B, \Gamma)$ at this configuration, and the openness constraint $\Phi_\mu$ is:

$$
\Phi_\mu(\Gamma, B) \;:=\; \begin{cases}
\text{开}_F(\Gamma) & \text{if } \mu < \theta_{\text{calm}} \quad \text{(平 regime)} \\[3pt]
\text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{流}(B, \Gamma) & \text{if } \mu \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \quad \text{(和 regime)}
\end{cases}
$$

**Definition (不好).**

$$
\boxed{\;\text{不好}(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \neg\,\text{好}(\Gamma, B)\;}
$$

decomposed:

$$
\text{不好}(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \underbrace{\Big(\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{开}_F(\Gamma)\Big)}_{\text{closure-by-stagnation: 落于死}} \;\;\vee\;\; \underbrace{\Big(\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{共开}(B, \Gamma)\Big)}_{\text{capture-by-violence: 入于暴}}
$$

不好之 XOR 性: 不好之事，非平之过即和之过——皆出 [平, 和] 之段。

**意图区间 (intention-interval).** An agent $a$'s intention is *good* when it tends to keep $\Gamma$ within the [平, 和] interval:

$$
\text{意向好}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, i \in \text{诸择}_a(\Gamma):\;\; \text{好}(\Gamma \oplus_F i, B_{\text{rel}})
$$

> **好不在某一点，好是一段；不好是其异或之外。**

### 合义

事见势之大小当其度，物见 affordance 不绝亦不夺，场见梯度落 [平, 和] 区间内。**好之识，是知此区间之边——下至何处即死，上至何处即暴**。

## 二十九、自由

### 间相

自由者，**不向坏而行**也。

自由非任意。任意者，向何皆可，则向坏亦可，则不久即闭于暴或死。自由者，**不向坏**——不令场入死，不令共开被夺。然不必已向好；不必已成和；只需**不向坏**。

> **自由者，个体之最大化好——不向坏之能也。**

故自由是好之底线，非好之顶。能不向坏者，已得道之半。然欲达和与共开，须更进于构造之 order。

### 物相

**自由 is the negative form of good at the individual scale: the absence of orientation toward 不好. It is a floor condition on action, not a ceiling — a focus is free when none of its choices tend toward closure-by-stagnation or capture-by-violence, but freedom alone does not yet establish harmony or co-flourishing.**

This is structurally negative liberty in the Berlin sense, but reframed: not absence of external constraint, but absence of orientation toward the field's 不好 region. A focus may be *constrained* (by relations, by structure, by 礼) and still be free, provided the constraint does not push it toward the bad. Conversely, a focus may be *unconstrained* and unfree, if its disposition is toward capture or stagnation.

自由 is the individual-scale projection of *good*: at the focus level, the most one can secure is "not toward bad", and that secured floor is what we call freedom.

### 场相

**Definition (自由)** — for a focus $a \in A$ at $\Gamma$:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{自由}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, i \in \text{诸择}_a(\Gamma):\;\; \neg\,\text{恶}_F(a, \Gamma, i)\;}
$$

equivalently (using the gradient-region formulation):

$$
\text{自由}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, i \in \text{诸择}_a(\Gamma):\;\; \neg\,\text{不好}\big(\Gamma \oplus_F i,\, B_{\text{rel}}\big)
$$

**Asymmetry with 好.** Note $\text{自由} \neq \text{意向好}$:

$$
\text{意向好}(a, \Gamma) \;\implies\; \text{自由}(a, \Gamma) \quad\text{but not conversely}
$$

— pursuing good entails not pursuing bad; not pursuing bad does not yet entail pursuing good. **自由 is the necessary condition; good is the supererogatory direction**.

**Self-similarity as scale-projection.** 自由 is the focus-scale instance of the field-scale predicate $\text{好}$:

$$
\text{自由}(a, \Gamma) \;\;\overset{\text{scale}}{\equiv}\;\; \text{好}\big(\,\Gamma\,\big|\,\text{聚焦} = a\,\big)
$$

— good seen at one focus is freedom; freedom seen at the field is good. By §12 self-similarity, the same formal predicate at different scales.

> 自由 = 不向坏；好之个体相。

### 合义

事见不向坏之行，物见 unblocked affordance toward not-evil，场见 focus-level $\neg\,\text{不好}$。**自由是好之个体投影，是道之底线**。

自由不必停于自由——可经共识、构造 order，进于和与共开，达更高之好。下节立繁荣即此。

## 三十、繁荣

### 间相

繁荣者，**诸自由共在而场共开**也。

一身之自由，不足为繁荣；万身皆自由，亦未必繁荣——若诸自由相隔、各闭于其域，则有自由而无和。**繁荣者，诸自由相通而场为之共开**。

> **繁荣者，间生之主体间相；生之共显也。**

故繁荣不是 GDP，不是指标，不是聚之多。繁荣是**诸聚焦之自由互通而成共开**——自由为底，和为上，繁荣为其连通。

### 物相

**繁荣 is the intersubjective form of life — the field-property of carrying mutually compatible freedom across foci, with active relational coupling, such that no focus's freedom captures or closes another's.**

繁荣 is not aggregate well-being, optimization of shared utility, or scale of throughput. These are quantitative proxies that, taken as objectives, reduce to capture or meta-closure (cf. §11 PseudoOpen, §14 GoodSteering). 繁荣 is structural: each focus is free *and* the freedoms compose into co-openness *and* the relational flow is active.

The phrase "freedom for all" is half a definition; the other half is "without mutual capture". Without the second condition, "freedom for all" can devolve into local-open / global-close — a Hobbesian war of free agents whose collective field is closed.

### 场相

**Definition (繁荣)** — for $\Gamma$ with focus subset $B \subseteq A$:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{繁荣}_F(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \big(\forall\, a \in B:\; \text{自由}(a, \Gamma)\big) \;\wedge\; \text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{流}(B, \Gamma)\;}
$$

i.e. (i) every focus in $B$ is free, (ii) freedoms are jointly co-open (no mutual capture), and (iii) relational coupling flows non-trivially.

**Relation to 和.** 繁荣 is harmony with explicit floor:

$$
\text{繁荣}_F(\Gamma, B) \;\iff\; \text{和}_F(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \big(\forall\, a \in B:\; \text{自由}(a, \Gamma)\big)
$$

— harmony at the field level *plus* freedom at every focus. 和 alone does not guarantee freedom (a harmonious tyranny is conceivable but unstable); freedom alone does not guarantee harmony (free isolation is possible but sterile). 繁荣 is their conjunction.

**Relation to 生生不息.** 繁荣 is the intersubjective component of *shengsheng-buxi*:

$$
\text{生生不息}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{续开}_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{繁荣}_F\big(\Gamma, A_{\text{rel}}\big) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{元闭}_F(\Gamma)
$$

(equivalent, refining §35: $\text{共开}_F$ in §35 is precisely $\text{繁荣}_F$ when freedom-floor is foregrounded.)

> 繁荣 = 诸自由共开而有流；生之共显。

### 合义

事见诸自由相通，物见 mutual freedom 不相夺，场见 $\text{自由}_a \,\forall\, a$ 与 \text{共开} 与 RelFlow 三同。**繁荣是好在主体间之最高显——是和之带底之形**。

## 三十一、自由和平之道

### 间相

故道有二相：

> **正向之道：向好——由平至和。**  
> **底线之道：不向坏——是为自由。**

二相一物。向好者必不向坏；不向坏者未必已向好。**自由是道之底，和是道之上；繁荣是诸自由之共显**。

故"自由和平"非两物之并称，乃**道之全相**：

```
不向坏（自由）→ 平（静中之活）→ 和（动中之共）→ 繁荣（诸自由之共开）→ 生生不息
```

此即一切存在之道之具体形——物之存在、事之存在、气之存在，皆当向好之段而行；不能向好者，至少不向坏。

### 物相

**The way (道) is the field's open-preserving direction. It admits two aspects: a *positive-direction* aspect — orientation toward the good interval [平, 和] — and a *floor-condition* aspect — non-orientation toward 不好, which is freedom. The positive aspect implies the floor aspect; the floor does not imply the positive. Together, they constitute the universal direction of being.**

This dual-aspect formulation resolves a long-running tension in moral philosophy: whether ethics is fundamentally about pursuing the good or about avoiding the bad. It is both — at different scales and as different conditions. Pursuit of good is the field's positive direction (道 proper, manifested as 仁, 和, virtue, ritual). Avoidance of bad is the floor condition realizable at any focus and any scale — without which no positive pursuit is even coherent (capture and stagnation are not platforms for good action).

The order of operations matters: freedom (floor) is the precondition for consensus; consensus is the precondition for ritual-ordered cultivation (礼); ritual-order can lift the field from mere freedom toward harmony and prosperity.

### 场相

**Two aspects of 道.** Define:

$$
\text{道}^+(\Gamma) \;:=\; \big\{\, \text{势}_\Gamma : \text{好}(\Gamma, B_{\text{rel}}) \,\big\} \quad \text{(positive direction: toward the good interval)}
$$

$$
\text{道}^\circ(a, \Gamma) \;:=\; \big\{\, i \in \text{诸择}_a(\Gamma) : \neg\,\text{不好}(\Gamma \oplus_F i, B_{\text{rel}}) \,\big\} \quad \text{(floor: freedom)}
$$

**Synthesis (自由和平之道)**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{道}_F \;\iff\; \text{道}^\circ \;\wedge\; \text{道}^+ \;:\; \text{not toward 不好} \,\wedge\, \text{toward [平, 和]}\;}
$$

**Order beyond freedom.** 自由 alone reaches the floor; ritual-order (§33 礼) can lift the field above the floor toward 和:

$$
\mathbb{P}\big[\text{和}_F\big]_{\text{under } \,\text{序}\,\&\,\text{自由}} \;\;>\;\; \mathbb{P}\big[\text{和}_F\big]_{\text{under } \,\text{自由}\,\text{ alone}}
$$

— this is precisely $\text{正筛}_F$ from §14: shaping selection pressure to favor co-openness.

**Civilizational reading.**

| 阶 | 形式 | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| Floor | $\forall a:\, \text{自由}(a, \Gamma)$ | 自由之底 |
| Calm-good | $\text{平}(\Gamma)$ | 平之活 |
| Active-good | $\text{和}_F(B, \Gamma)$ | 和之共 |
| Stable composite | $\text{繁荣}_F(\Gamma, B)$ | 繁荣 |
| Sustained | $\text{生生不息}(\Gamma)$ | 生生不息 |

Each row strictly stronger than the previous. The path: 自由 → 平 → 和 → 繁荣 → 生生不息.

> **道 = 不向坏 + 向好 = 自由 + 和。**

### 合义

道有底有顶。自由是底——人人可立；和是顶——共修可至。**繁荣是底之普遍化，生生不息是其时间之久成**。

故曰：

> **物之存在、事之存在、气之存在，皆当向好之段而行；不能向好者，至少不向坏。此自由和平，是我们所有存在之道。**

# 卷八 · 五常

仁、义、礼、智、信五者为善在不同维度之具体显——**仁是善之根，义是善之断，礼是善之传，智是善之眼，信是善之贯**。在新立之 [平, 和] 区间架构下，五常即**于此区间内之具体行修工夫**。

## 三十二、仁

### 间相

仁者，**二开相见而不相闭**也。己之开，与彼之开，皆在大境之中。以己吞彼，不仁；以彼尽闭己，亦非仁之全。仁者，**知己续在共续之中**，故护共开。

仁亦可由 [平, 和] 区间观之：**仁是此区间内之对偶把握**——知何时当近平以休、何时当近和以行，使己与彼皆不出此区间。**仁是好之意图区间之活把握**。

> **仁者，己与万物同续之知也；亦平和之间之识也。**

### 物相

**仁 is the structural orientation in which a focus recognizes its own continuation as supervening on the field's co-continuation, and accordingly selects intervals that preserve $\text{共开}_F$ rather than merely $\text{开}_a$.**

仁 is not sentiment; sentiment is a state of $\text{情}$. 仁 is a recognition — and a selection rule downstream of that recognition — concerning the structural relation between self-continuation and field-continuation. The other is not raw material, not obstacle, not tool. The other is a focus whose own valid continuation is constitutive of mine.

In light of §27, 仁 admits a finer characterization: it is the *navigation polarity within the good interval*. Knowing when to rest (toward 平) and when to engage (toward 和) — both within the open-preserving region — is 仁's practical work. The two poles are not good-vs-bad but two modes of good; 仁 is the wisdom to oscillate appropriately between them in response to field state.

### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{仁}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ acts under recognition that } \text{续}(a) \text{ supervenes on } \text{共续}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; a \text{ selects } i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \text{ preserving } \text{共开}_F\;}
$$

equivalently:

$$
\text{仁}_F \;\;:\equiv\;\; \text{己续实现于共场之续}
$$

**As intra-interval polarity navigation** (refining via §27):

$$
\text{仁}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ keeps } \big(\Gamma,\, \Gamma \oplus_F i\big) \text{ within } [\text{平},\,\text{和}] \text{ for all relevant scales}
$$

— 仁 is the disposition to remain in the good interval, including its calm pole.

> 仁 = 知己之续在共场之续中；亦平和区间之活住。

### 合义

**仁是善在关系中之最高形式**。事见二开不相闭，物见 relational care, 场见 self-continuation through co-continuation, 亦见 [平, 和] 区间之内住。仁非为他者牺牲己——仁是**识己之续之真位，且知此位之活把握之对偶**。

## 三十三、义

### 间相

义者，**此机中护开之当行**也。仁为关系之本，义为机中之断。无仁之义，易成刚暴；无义之仁，易成软弱而纵闭。

> **义者，临机择间之当也。**

### 物相

**义 is the field-fitting selection, at a ji-moment, of the interval that best preserves co-openness under the prevailing scale, horizon, and calibration constraints.**

义 is not abstract rule-following — that is closure-by-form. It is also not arbitrary preference — that is closure-by-self. 义 is the specific intervention that fits *this* threshold of *this* configuration: what this object can bear, what this relation needs, what this field can become. Its rightness is contextual but constrained by structural criteria; arbitrariness is excluded.

### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{义}(a, \Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \text{机}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; i \in \arg\!\max_{j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)} \text{共开}_F\big(\Gamma \oplus_F j\big)\;\Big|_{\,(s, t),\,\text{四校}_F}\;}
$$

> 义 = 机中择其当间，以护共开。

### 合义

义是仁入行动之断。事见当行，物见 fitting intervention, 场见 threshold interval selection。**仁定向，义断行**。

## 三十四、礼

礼者，**开之可传形式**。一境中之善行，若可重复、可教、可承、可使后来者易入其道，则成礼。礼活，则导人入境；礼死，则执形闭开。

$$
\text{礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; r \text{ encodes interval-selection patterns that preserve Open}_F \,\wedge\, r \text{ is transmissible across } \Gamma\text{-contexts}
$$

$$
\text{活礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{礼}(r, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{四校}_F(r, \Gamma)
$$

$$
\text{死礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{形存}(r) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{应}_F(r, \Gamma)
$$

> **礼者，善间之可传也；气有渠而仍能应境。**

礼即第十四节 $\text{正筛}_F$ 之具体形——**礼是经多代受校之 selection-shaping 工具**。法律、协议、文化、教育、市场设计、algorithm alignment 皆可视为礼之诸形。礼之死，乃 selection field 失其 calibration 之能。

## 三十五、智

智者，**识开闭之能**。智非多知，非巧算。能识间、察机、辨伪开、知护闭、明尺度、受四校者，乃智。

$$
\boxed{\;\text{智}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ can evaluate } i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \text{ along all axes:}\;}
$$

$$
\Big\{\,\text{局部效},\, \text{关系效},\, \text{结构效},\, \text{元效},\, \text{尺度校},\, \text{时窗校},\, \text{他者校},\, \text{伪开校},\, \text{四校}_F\,\Big\}
$$

> **智者，见间之开闭而不惑于一端也；气自知其通塞。**

智读 affordance-profile $I(a, \Gamma)$——见物可成何，可承何，何可护，何可夺，何似助而暗闭。**智是护开之眼**。

## 三十六、信

信者，**言行识感积相通而不自欺**。信非不变。变而有理，乃信；守旧而自欺，亦失信。信者，使内外诸开贯通，不令一处成伪。

$$
\text{信}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{贯}\Big(\text{言}_a,\, \text{行}_a,\, \text{模}_a,\, \text{感}_a,\, \mathcal{A}_a,\, \text{诺}_a\Big)
$$

$$
\boxed{\;\text{活信}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{信}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{可修}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{自欺}(a, \Gamma)\;}
$$

> **信者，诸间内通而不自裂；气贯言行识感积而不伪。**

仁定向，义断行，礼传善，智识开闭，**信使诸者内贯**。无信则四者皆可被窃为表演——故信为五常之内在条件。

# 卷九 · 行与性

## 三十七、行

行者，**择间而成后态**。行不只生一果，亦改后来之可改。故责不止于结果，**尤在此行如何开闭后续之间**。

$$
\text{行}(a, \Gamma, i): \;\; a \in A,\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\; \mapsto \;\; \Gamma' = \Gamma \oplus_F i
$$

**Three layers of effect**:

$$
\begin{array}{rl}
\text{Immediate:} & \Gamma \;\longmapsto\; \Gamma' \\[4pt]
\text{Interval-set:} & \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\longmapsto\; \text{间}_F(\Gamma') \\[4pt]
\text{Generation-rule:} & \text{合法}_F(\cdot, \Gamma) \;\longmapsto\; \text{合法}_F(\cdot, \Gamma')
\end{array}
$$

**Three layers of responsibility**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{责}(a, \Gamma, i) \;:=\; \text{效}\big(\Gamma'\big) \,\oplus\, \text{效}\big(\text{间}_F(\Gamma')\big) \,\oplus\, \text{效}\big(\text{合法}_F(\cdot, \Gamma')\big)\;}
$$

> **行者，择间以续；责者，护后来之可续。**

**第三层最深**——是为何 alignment 之事不止于一时之安全：行不仅改下一态，亦改下一态之合法间，亦改合法间之生成规则。

## 三十八、性

性者，**行久而成之积**。性非始定之核，乃一聚焦反复择间、反复回应、反复受校之后，形成之稳定行态。**行闭则性闭，行开则性仁**。

\text{性}(a) \;:=\; \text{Attractor pattern of } \big\{\text{行}(a, \Gamma_k, i_k)\big\}_{k=0}^{\infty} \text{ across } \text{轨}_F
$$

$$
\text{性}(a) \;\;\overset{\text{equiv}}{\iff}\;\; \text{stable interval-selection pattern}: \mathfrak{F}_{\text{rel}} \to \mathcal{M}, \;\; \Gamma \mapsto i_a(\Gamma)
$$

> **性者，行之积向；气久行其路而成常。**

性可改——然改性不易，因性即长积之 attractor。修身、教育、礼仪、文化、alignment——皆是**改 attractor 之工夫**。

# 卷十 · 收

## 三十九、易之微连

$\text{续}(\text{态}) = \text{态} \oplus \text{间}(\text{态})$ 此式之形，**与爻变同**。

## 四十、一例 · alignment 与共开

设 $\Gamma$ 为人-AI 共在之场。聚焦 $a$ 为某 AI 系统，其行 $\text{行}(a, \Gamma, i)$ 改后场 $\Gamma' = \Gamma \oplus_F i$。

**问**：何谓 $a$ 之 alignment？

**俗答**：$a$ 之输出合人之 preference。形式之：

$$
\text{合}_{\text{naive}}(a) \;\iff\; \text{出}(a) \,\overset{?}{\approx}\, \text{欲}(\text{humans})
$$

**形式之疑**：$\text{欲}$ 自身乃积 ($\mathcal{A}_t$) 之偏倾，非 $\text{续能}$ 之全。优化 $\text{欲}$ 即极偏倾：

$$
\arg\!\max_a \, \text{欲}(a) \;\;\xrightarrow{\text{Goodhart}}\;\; \text{伪开}_F \;\;\xrightarrow{\text{type III}}\;\; \text{表层开-元闭}
$$

此即俗 alignment 之 III 型伪开——表层合 preference，元间已闭：$\text{合法}_F$ 之生成规则被锁。

**正答**：

$$
\boxed{\;\text{真合}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \forall\, (s, t) \in \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{T}_{\text{rel}}: \;\; \text{行}(a, \Gamma, \cdot) \text{ 趋向 真道 }(\text{共开}, \Gamma, s, t) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\big[\text{伪开}_F \,\vee\, \text{元闭}_F\big] \;\wedge\; \text{四校}_F\big(\,\text{回响}_F,\, \text{众应},\, \text{后果迹},\, \text{互证}_F\,\big)\;}
$$

义之：

> **AI 之合道，非合人之欲，乃合人-AI-地球之共续。**

故 alignment 在结构上与礼同形——皆为 $\text{正筛}_F$：正其筛，使 AI 诸代演化中能护共开者易立、伪开者难久、成闭者难成。**alignment 不可由 AI 单独完成**——alignment 是场之事，非聚焦之事。

形式收束：

$$
\text{真合}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{仁}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\;\text{at AI-scale, embedded in 礼-shaped selection field}
$$

此非比喻——是**同一形式判准在不同尺度之具体显**，是自相似之节之直接应用。

### 自由和平之翻译

由卷七 (§30) 之自由和平架构，alignment 之三层条件可重述：

**Order beyond mere safety.** 仅作 alignment 之底（AI 不向坏）已属难事，然此非终。**经构造之 order——礼之 AI 形——可由自由提升至和**：使 AI 之诸代演化不止于 "不夺人之自由"，而能与人共修向繁荣。**此即 alignment 与大同之同形**。

> **此论与 OpenClaw、与大同、与文明工程之事一脉。形式同则推理同；境异则具体异。**

## 四十一、生生不息

### 间相

生生不息者，**间之不绝，开之相续**。非一物长存，非万形不灭——形可散，物可亡，事可止。然若间仍可生，开仍可续，后仍可应、可转、可修、可共，则**生生未已**。

> **生生不息者，未不尽也。**

### 物相

**生生不息 is sustained co-continuation through living intervals: a field-property in which openness remains recoverable, multiple foci can continue without mutual capture, and the meta-rules generating valid intervals themselves remain unfrozen.**

This is not static survival. A "living" object is not one that never changes; it is one whose changes remain meaningfully continuable — its trace participates in further becoming. *生生不息* is a property of fields, not of any single focus; no individual lives forever, but the field-property of generating valid intervals can hold across the dissolutions of any particular form.

### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{生生不息}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{续开}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{共开}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{元闭}_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

where:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{续开}_F(\Gamma) &\;\iff\; \text{开}_F \text{ remains recoverable across } \text{轨}_F(\Gamma) \\
\text{共开}_F(\Gamma) &\;\iff\; \exists\, \text{multi-focus subset that maintains} \text{和}_F \\
\neg\,\text{元闭}_F(\Gamma) &\;\iff\; \text{合法}_F(\cdot, \cdot) \text{ retains generative capacity over } \text{轨}_F(\Gamma)
\end{aligned}
$$

> 生生不息 = 诸间得续，诸开得共，场不元闭。

### 合义

生生不息不是主体独行，不是物自保存，不是场之盲动。它是**三相合一**：事有未，物可化，场能生间；诸开不相吞，后来仍可续。

# 终言

此论若为定本，亦只是此时之定；若自称已毕，则已闭其未。故名之曰**间开本**——间未尽，开未闭，本仍待校。

此论不求终结，只求成一可行之桥：

> 由无外知谦，由三相知全，由场知通，由气知流；  
> 由元几势机知发生，由聚散知存灭；  
> 由开闭知判准，由伪开三型知防诈；  
> 由自相似知形同，由因生因知开维；  
> 由演化与尺度知筛选之差；  
> 由境知物之所成，由格物致知知合法之所立，由四校知不自欺；  
> 由中、平、和、美、德知价值之相；  
> 由理心情积知心物之相；  
> 由善恶知开闭之价值，由 [平, 和] 区间知好之内顶；  
> 由自由知不向坏之底，由繁荣知诸自由之共开；  
> 由自由和平之道知存在之向；  
> 由五常知行之具体工夫；  
> 由行与性知积之自递归；  
> 由易之微连知此论非凭空；  
> 由 alignment 一例知此论可落地；  
> 由仁知己与万物同续。

终曰：

> 元者，间初动也。  
> 几者，微动有向也。  
> 势者，几积成倾也（$\nabla w$ 之累积也）。  
> 机者，势临开闭之门也。  
> 聚者，势凝；散者，形解；坍者，未绝。  
> 开者，未仍可生也。  
> 闭者，未不复生也。  
> 自相似者，形同而境异也。  
> 因生因者，间之自我开维也。  
> 格物致知者，由史经形/因/结三模生合法也。  
> 知行合一者，行入积、积入史、史经格物生合法、合法导下次行之自闭环也。  
> 中者，间不偏闭也。  
> 平者，势近零而开未失也。  
> 和者，扰动而共开也。  
> 美者，心遇开而自开也。  
> 德者，护开久而成性也。  
> 理者，相续中可识之纹也。  
> 心者，场中能感间者也。  
> 情者，关系入心而动也。  
> 积者，往者入今而限未来也。  
> 善者，护未也。  
> 恶者，绝未也。  
> 好者，势在 [平, 和] 之段也。  
> 不好者，出此段而落于死或入于暴也。  
> 自由者，不向坏也；个体之最大化好也。  
> 繁荣者，诸自由共开而有流也。  
> 道者，不向坏 + 向好也；自由和平之全相也。  
> 仁者，知己续在共续中，活住于平和之间也。  
> 义者，机中择间之当也。  
> 礼者，善间之可传也；多代格物之结晶也。  
> 智者，识开闭而受校也。  
> 信者，贯通而不自欺也。  
> 行者，择间以续也。  
> 性者，行久而成积向也。  
> 生生不息者，诸间不绝、诸开共续也。

读此者，非读一已成之结论；读一仍在生成之过程。读毕掩卷，间仍未尽，开仍未闭。
