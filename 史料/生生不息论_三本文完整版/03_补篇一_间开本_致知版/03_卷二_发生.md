## 卷二 · 发生

发生五节——元、几、势、机、聚散——皆三相全显。此五者为间生之骨架，缺一则发生之论不全。

### 五、元

#### 间相

元者，间之初显为动也。未可名物，未可定形，而动机已露；无所从来，非有所发；间不自闭，故微动生焉。

元非第一物，非第一因。执元为本，则问元与非元何分；分处即间。**故元赖间而名，非先间而立**。

> **元者，间初动也；非本，乃端。**

#### 物相

**元 denotes the threshold at which a possibility within a field acquires a faint but detectable presence — prior to becoming a stable object of recognition.**

It is not a substance, not an origin-point, and not the first member of an enumeration. From the object-side, 元 is the earliest distinguishable trace by which something may *later* appear as a stable focus. Before an object is an object, the field already holds latent affordances shaped by constraints, history, and relation; 元 is the moment one of these latencies crosses into the regime of being noticeable, without yet being noticed-as-something.

In one phrase: *yuan is object-genesis, not object-substance.*

#### 场相

**Definition (元).**

$$
\text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\iff\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; w_\Gamma(i) > \varepsilon \;\wedge\; \neg\, \text{稳焦}(i, \Gamma)
$$

i.e. $i$ is a valid interval whose activation has crossed the manifestation threshold $\varepsilon$, but whose pattern has not yet stabilized.

**Trace condition** (immediate corollary):

$$
\text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\implies\; \text{迹}(i, \Gamma) := \int_{\text{轨}_F^{(n)}} \mathbb{1}[w_{\Gamma_k}(i) > \varepsilon] \,dk \;>\; 0
$$

元 leaves an integrable trace, but does not yet form a stable attractor.

> 元 = 有效场间之最小激活，已越显化阈而未成稳。

#### 合义

事言其动，物言其迹，场言其激活。元非始物，非始因，乃**场中一间初动而将显为物之端**。

### 六、几

#### 间相

几者，元动而未形也。元为动端，几为微著。一几难识，众几相续，其痕自显。识几者，非见物，乃**见未形之向**；非见果，乃见将成之机。

> **几者，间之微著，势之未成也。**

#### 物相

**Ji denotes the earliest readable directional bias in a becoming — an inflection of the field's activation that points toward a particular kind of formation, while the formation itself remains unrealized.**

A single 几 may lie below resolution. 几 becomes legible when its directional component accumulates across an orbit and begins to differentiate between possible futures. 几 is therefore inseparable from observation-at-scale: at one scale the field is noise; at another, the same field carries a discernible drift.

#### 场相

**Definition (几).** A valid interval $i$ at field state $\Gamma$ exhibits 几 iff its activation has begun to acquire a directional gradient component but has not yet stabilized:

$$
\text{几}(i, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{元}(i, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \big\| \nabla_i w_\Gamma \big\| > \delta \;\wedge\; w_\Gamma(i) < \tau_{\text{stab}}
$$

**Orbit-readability of 几**. Over an orbit segment $\text{轨}_F^{(n)}(\Gamma_0)$, 几 is readable when the directional component accumulates above the bias threshold:

$$
\text{可读之几}(j, \text{轨}_F^{(n)}) \;\iff\; \Bigg\| \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \nabla_j w_{\Gamma_k} \Bigg\| \;>\; \delta_{\text{read}}
$$

i.e. the gradient along $j$, integrated along the orbit, exceeds the readability threshold.

> 几 = 场续中微露之向，未成势，已非全无。

#### 合义

间相见微动，物相见 proto-affordance，场相见 nascent gradient。**几为势之种，势为几之积**。

### 七、势

#### 间相

势者，几相续而成倾也。一几未足成势；众几相续，其向渐明。

势非力——力有施受；势非物——物有定形。势者，**诸微之相续自成其倾**。势有方向，无终极目的。方向者，已显之倾；目的者，预设之终。**执方向为目的，则以一端闭万端**。

> **势者，几之积向也。**

#### 物相

**势 is the gradient field of activation density on the interval space — the structured propensity that biases which intervals are most likely to be activated next.**

势 is not a force imposed from outside, nor a particle of energy localized in some object. It is the *shape of the activation landscape itself*: the cumulative directional bias that an orbit has acquired through its own trajectory. From the object-side, 势 manifests as inertia, drift, structural momentum, or developmental tendency — but these manifestations are projections of an underlying gradient field on $\text{间}_F(\Gamma)$.

The Sun-Tzu image of a drawn crossbow or held-back water captures this exactly: 势 is potential gradient, not actualized motion.

#### 场相

**Definition (势 / 势 — instantaneous).** 势 at a field state $\Gamma$ is the gradient of activation weight on the interval space:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{势}_\Gamma \;:=\; \nabla w_\Gamma \;\in\; T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

a covector field assigning to each interval direction $j$ the local rate of change $\nabla_j w_\Gamma$.

**Definition (势 / 势 — orbit-accumulated).** Along an orbit segment $\text{轨}_F^{(n)}(\Gamma_0) = (\Gamma_0, \ldots, \Gamma_n)$, the accumulated 势 is the orbit-integrated gradient:

$$
\boxed{\;\bar{\text{势}}\big(\text{轨}_F^{(n)}\big) \;:=\; \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \nabla w_{\Gamma_k} \;\in\; \bigoplus_{k=0}^{n-1} T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma_k)\;}
$$

**Pairing with a direction.** Given a candidate interval $j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma_n)$, the directional 势 along $j$ is

$$
\bar{\text{势}}_n(j) \;:=\; \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} \big\langle \nabla w_{\Gamma_k},\, \pi_k(j) \big\rangle
$$

where $\pi_k$ is the natural projection of $j$ onto $T^*\text{间}_F(\Gamma_k)$ (well-defined when $j$ has a coherent representative across the orbit).

**Continuum limit.** When $\text{轨}_F$ admits a continuous parametrization $\Gamma : [0, T] \to \mathfrak{F}$, the accumulated 势 becomes the path integral:

$$
\bar{\text{势}}\big(\Gamma_{[0,T]}\big) \;=\; \int_0^T \nabla w_{\Gamma_\tau} \, d\tau
$$

> 势 = 间空间上权重之梯度场；轨道之 $\nabla w$ 沿路径之累积。

#### 合义

几为局部梯度（$\nabla w$ 之初显），势为累积梯度（$\bar{\text{势}}$）。**势之既成，未必显为物，然必显为后续之偏倾**——即下一步 $\Gamma_{n+1}$ 之选择被 $\bar{\text{势}}$ 所偏。

### 八、机

#### 间相

机者，势之临界也。势至其转处，开闭将分而未分，可行可失，谓之机。机非钟表之刻——机非外时之点；机非时间中一物——机乃**势中张力之内门**。

> **机者，势临开闭之门也。**

#### 物相

**Ji-moment is the structural threshold at which the field's openness profile bifurcates: two distinct continuations from the same state lead to qualitatively different open futures.**

Ji-moment is not "a moment in time" in the chronometric sense. It is a topological feature of the orbit space: a state from which paths diverge in their open-preserving capacity. Two intervals out of the same field state, both formally valid, lead to non-equivalent openness profiles downstream. Where such divergence exists, action matters in a way that mere passage of time does not capture.

#### 场相

**Definition (开度剖面 / Openness profile).** Define $\text{开度剖面}(\Gamma) := \big(\text{未}_F, \text{可}_F, \text{应}_F, \text{转}_F\big)(\Gamma) \in \{0,1\}^4$ — the four-dimensional binary profile of openness components evaluated at $\Gamma$. (See §10 for the components.)

**Definition (机).**

$$
\boxed{\;\text{机}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \exists\, i \neq j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma): \;\; \text{开度剖面}(\Gamma \oplus_F i) \;\neq\; \text{开度剖面}(\Gamma \oplus_F j)\;}
$$

**Necessity vs sufficiency.** $|\text{间}_F(\Gamma)| \geq 2$ is *necessary* but not *sufficient*: divergence of choices alone is not enough — the divergence must produce non-equivalent openness profiles. If all $\Gamma \oplus_F i$ for $i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)$ share the same $\text{开度剖面}$, the state is choice-rich but not a 机.

**Action-availability of a 机**. For an agent-focus $a \in A$:

$$
\text{可行之机}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{机}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \exists\, i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma):\; \text{行}(a, \Gamma, i) \text{ is realizable}
$$

> 机 = 场间分歧之处，择一间则开闭异路。

#### 合义

势为累积梯度，机为分歧门。**于机失行，则势自决之；于机识间而行，乃义之初显**。义之论待二十四节。

### 九、聚散

#### 间相

聚者，势之凝；散者，形之解。聚非凭空成物，散非彻底归无。形散而迹存，迹入后续而成积。然若不变量俱断，所开之路俱闭，则**死与坍缩真实**——非生之另一相，乃间之真绝。

> **聚散者，势与形之往复也。死者，聚散之极端，亦真有。**

#### 物相

**Gathering and dispersal are the formation and dissolution of stable predicate-truths along an orbit. Real collapse is the special case of dispersal in which no continuation predicate remains supported.**

An object gathers when a predicate $P$ becomes stably true under the field's dynamics. It disperses when this stability is lost. Dispersal need not be annihilation: residues, constraints, memories, and trace-affordances may seed later formations. But some dispersals are real collapse — the predicate fails *and* no successor predicate inherits its constraints into a continuable structure.

#### 场相

Let $P : \mathfrak{F} \to \{0, 1\}$ be a predicate over field states. Let $B_s(\Gamma)$ denote the scale-$s$ orbit neighborhood of $\Gamma$.

**Form / 聚 (gathering).**

$$
\text{形}(P, \Gamma, s) \;\iff\; P(\Gamma) = 1 \;\wedge\; \forall\, \Gamma' \in B_s(\Gamma):\; P(\Gamma') = 1
$$

**Dispersal / 散.**

$$
\text{散}(P, \Gamma_n) \;\iff\; \exists\, m > n,\;\; \forall\, k \geq m:\; P(\Gamma_k) = 0
$$

**Trace transmission.** Let $\text{不变}(P)$ denote the structural invariants of $P$ (parameters, constraints, attractor signature). Then transformation between predicates:

$$
\text{化}(P \to Q) \;\iff\; \text{不变}(P) \subseteq \text{条件}(Q) \;\vee\; \text{不变}(P) \text{ enters } \mathcal{C}(\Gamma_{>m})
$$

**Real collapse / 真坍缩.**

$$
\boxed{\;\text{真坍}(P, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{散}(P, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \neg\, \exists\, Q:\; \text{化}(P \to Q)\;}
$$

— predicate dies *and* its invariants enter no successor.

> 聚 = 场谓之暂稳；散 = 场谓之解稳；真坍缩 = 解稳无续。

#### 合义

聚散非二事，乃同一场续中稳定与解稳之交替。**死与坍缩为真——此论不诡辩生灭一相而忽视真绝**。能否于他场显其迹，乃聚散之分水。
