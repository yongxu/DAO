## 卷五 · 价值之相

中、和、美、德四节皆三相全显。此四者为开闭在不同维度之具体显，是**价值之根**——非外加规范，乃护开之自然之态。

### 十八、中

#### 间相

中者，**不使开闭于一端**也。中非中点，非折衷，非平均。中者，识一境之对偶张力，使两端之开皆不被一端吞没。必要时，中亦能护闭一处，以护更大之开。

> **中者，间不偏闭也。**

#### 物相

**中 is the dynamic field-state in which a polarity's two poles each retain live continuation; neither has captured the other.**

A field in 中 is not statistically centered between two extremes — that is a category error. *Zhong* is a structural property of polarity-openness: the field's continuation capacity remains live along *both* polar dimensions, even when activation is currently asymmetric. *Zhong* admits unilateral protective closure when one pole's wider openness depends on the other being temporarily restrained.

#### 场相

For a polarity $\mathfrak{d} = (u, v)$ on $\Gamma$:

**Pole-extremity**:

$$
\text{极}_u(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \big\| \nabla w_\Gamma \big\|_u \gg \big\| \nabla w_\Gamma \big\|_v \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{开}^v_F(\Gamma)
$$

**Definition (中)**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{中}_F(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \neg\,\text{极}_u(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{极}_v(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \,\wedge\, \text{开}^u_F(\Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{开}^v_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

equivalently:

$$
\text{中}_F(\Gamma, \mathfrak{d}) \;\iff\; \forall\, x \in \{u, v\}:\; \text{开}^x_F(\Gamma) \;\text{is not closed beyond repair by activation at } x^c
$$

> 中 = 场中对偶不被一端吞没之动态。

#### 合义

事见其不极，物见其多 affordance 可活，场见其 polarity-openness 得保。**中是开在对偶中之形式**。

### 十九、平

#### 间相

平者，**场无扰动而仍开**也。

势小而场不躁，气定而隙未绝。平非死寂——死寂者，无扰动而开尽闭；平非僵静——僵静者，似无变而生意已断。**平者，活之未发，未必发之活**。

平为众动之底——一切扰动皆自平起，亦终归于平。然平之为平，不在无扰动，在**有扰动而能复归**。

> **平者，势近零而开未失也。**

#### 物相

**平 is the field-state of vanishing gradient — minimal perturbation in the activation landscape — coupled with sustained openness. It is not stillness as death; it is rest as readiness.**

Without the openness condition, low gradient signals collapse: a field with $\nabla w \to 0$ and $\neg\,\text{开}_F$ is a closed field, dead matter, terminal absorption. 平 is the conjunction — calm *and* alive. From the object-side, 平 is a system at low excitation but with intact response capacity: a poised configuration whose disturbance would generate response, repair, and revision.

平 is the floor of the good region: the lowest sustainable perturbation that is still alive.

#### 场相

**Definition (平)** ——

$$
\boxed{\;\text{平}(\Gamma) \;\iff\; \big\| \text{势}_\Gamma \big\| \;<\; \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma)\;}
$$

i.e. the magnitude of the activation gradient is below the calm threshold, *and* the openness predicate holds.

**Distinction from closure.** With $\theta_{\text{calm}}$ as the calm threshold:

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \text{开}_F(\Gamma) &\Longrightarrow\; \text{平}(\Gamma) \quad \text{(calm-alive)} \\[3pt]
\big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{开}_F(\Gamma) &\Longrightarrow\; \text{闭}_F(\Gamma) \quad \text{(calm-dead)}
\end{array}
$$

低梯度本身不分善恶；分善恶者，是否仍能开。

**Recoverability** (one direction of 平's liveness):

$$
\text{平}(\Gamma) \;\implies\; \exists\, \delta\text{-perturbation: } \Gamma \rightsquigarrow \Gamma',\;\; \big\|\text{势}_{\Gamma'}\big\| > \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma')
$$

— an open-calm field can be perturbed back into active openness, i.e. into 和 (next section).

> 平 = 梯度近零之活态。

#### 合义

事见无扰之续，物见 poised configuration，场见 $\nabla w \to 0$ 而 $\text{开}_F$ 不失。**平是开在静中之形式**。

平、和为对偶之二极，**皆在好之区间内**——下节即立此区间。

### 二十、和

#### 间相

和者，**诸开相通而不相吞**也。和非同——同者以一吞多；和非斗——斗者以己闭彼；和非隔——隔者各守其闭。和者，差异仍在，而诸开相通，互生而不相夺。

> **和者，各开而互通也。**

#### 物相

**和 is the relational composition of multiple foci such that each retains its openness, the openness profiles are mutually compatible, and there is non-zero relational coupling between them.**

*He* is not sameness (which is uniformity by absorption) and not mere coexistence (which can be openness-without-relation, i.e. isolation). *He* requires three things simultaneously: each focus open, their open-profiles compatible, and live coupling among them. Difference must remain — but as participation, not as wall.

#### 场相

For a focus subset $B \subseteq A$ on $\Gamma$:

**Co-openness**:

$$
\text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \big(\forall\, a \in B:\; \text{开}_a(\Gamma)\big) \,\wedge\, \text{相合}\big(\{\text{开}_a\}_{a \in B}, \Gamma\big) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{夺}(B, \Gamma)
$$

**Relational flow**:

$$
\text{流}(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \exists\, a \neq b \in B:\; \kappa(\mathcal{I}_a, \mathcal{I}_b) \neq 0 \;\wedge\; \text{the coupling is not capture-typed}
$$

**Definition (和)**:

$$
\boxed{\;\text{和}_F(B, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \big\| \text{势}_\Gamma \big\| \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{流}(B, \Gamma)\;}
$$

i.e. perturbation present (gradient above the calm threshold), but distributed across foci such that openness is jointly preserved and relational coupling flows non-trivially.

> 和 = 有扰而共开，且诸开互通。

**Polarity with 平.** 平 and 和 are the two sustainable poles of the good region:

$$
\begin{array}{ll}
\text{平}(\Gamma) & : \;\; \big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| < \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{开}_F(\Gamma) \\[3pt]
\text{和}_F(B, \Gamma) & : \;\; \big\|\text{势}_\Gamma\big\| \geq \theta_{\text{calm}} \;\wedge\; \text{共开}(B, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{流}(B, \Gamma)
\end{array}
$$

二者皆在好之内，分于扰动之有无。

#### 合义

中治对偶之极，平治静而活，和治动而共。**中、平、和三者，开在不同维度之三显**。下卷立 [平, 和] 区间为好。

### 二十一、美

#### 间相

美者，**心遇开而自开**也。心遇一物、一景、一声、一人，若其间被引动，其未被唤醒，其感应开一新维，则谓之美。美非利，非快，非正——**美者，开感也**。

> **美者，开与开相遇而互显也。**

#### 物相

**Beauty is the encounter-event between a sensing focus and an object-focus in which the sensing focus's openness profile is enlarged, without inducing closure elsewhere in the field.**

Beauty is not utility (which subordinates the object to a need), not pleasure (which can be a closed loop), and not normative correctness (which can be empty form). It is the disclosure of latent affordance-depth in the encountered, registered as an enlargement of openness in the encountering. Deep beauty further increases the *relational* openness between the two — not merely the perceiver's private state.

#### 场相

For a sensing focus $m$ encountering an object focus $o$ at field state $\Gamma$:

**Beauty**:

$$
\text{美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{遇}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\implies\; \Big[\text{开}_m(\Gamma_+) > \text{开}_m(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{成闭}_F(\Gamma_+)\Big]
$$

where $\Gamma_+ = \Gamma \oplus_F i_{\text{enc}}$ is the post-encounter field.

**Deep beauty** (relational enlargement):

$$
\text{深美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{美}(m, o, \Gamma) \;\wedge\; \text{关开}(m, o, \Gamma_+) > \text{关开}(m, o, \Gamma)
$$

> 美 = 相遇而开，且不闭其场；深美 = 关系亦开。

#### 合义

事见心开，物见 affordance depth，场见 encounter 改变 openness profile。**美是开在感应中之形式**。

### 二十二、德

#### 间相

德者，**护开之积**也。一行合中，不足谓德；久行而积，遇境自能感其机、守其中、致其和、辨其伪开、用其护闭，乃谓德。

> **德者，护开久而成性也。**

#### 物相

**德 is a focus's stable disposition toward open-preserving action, distributed across a wide range of contexts, and remaining responsive rather than rigid.**

Virtue is not an abstract rule, not an inner essence, and not a memorized behavior. It is the orbit-level signature of a focus whose interval-selection consistently preserves openness across diverse $\Gamma$. Living virtue is calibratable: it updates under the four-fold check rather than ossifying into form. Rigidity is closure-disguised-as-virtue.

#### 场相

For an agent-focus $a$:

**Virtue**:

$$
\text{德}(a) \;\iff\; \forall\, \Gamma \in \mathfrak{F}_{\text{rel}}:\; \mathbb{P}\Big[\, \text{行}(a, \Gamma, i) \text{ preserves Open}_F \,\Big] \;\geq\; \theta_{\text{virtue}}
$$

**True virtue** (calibratable & non-rigid):

$$
\text{真德}(a) \;\iff\; \text{德}(a) \;\wedge\; \text{四校}_F(a, \cdot) \;\wedge\; \neg\,\text{僵}(a)
$$

> 德 = 场中反复护开之行，沉淀为稳定回应；真德兼受四校。

#### 合义

事见行久，物见 disposition，场见 tendency 被稳定化。**德是开在时间积累中之形式**。中、和、美、德四者互显——和必含美，美必含中，德必含中而显于和与美之能。
