## 卷八 · 五常

仁、义、礼、智、信五者为善在不同维度之具体显——**仁是善之根，义是善之断，礼是善之传，智是善之眼，信是善之贯**。在新立之 [平, 和] 区间架构下，五常即**于此区间内之具体行修工夫**。

### 三十二、仁

#### 间相

仁者，**二开相见而不相闭**也。己之开，与彼之开，皆在大境之中。以己吞彼，不仁；以彼尽闭己，亦非仁之全。仁者，**知己续在共续之中**，故护共开。

仁亦可由 [平, 和] 区间观之：**仁是此区间内之对偶把握**——知何时当近平以休、何时当近和以行，使己与彼皆不出此区间。**仁是好之意图区间之活把握**。

> **仁者，己与万物同续之知也；亦平和之间之识也。**

#### 物相

**仁 is the structural orientation in which a focus recognizes its own continuation as supervening on the field's co-continuation, and accordingly selects intervals that preserve $\text{共开}_F$ rather than merely $\text{开}_a$.**

仁 is not sentiment; sentiment is a state of $\text{情}$. 仁 is a recognition — and a selection rule downstream of that recognition — concerning the structural relation between self-continuation and field-continuation. The other is not raw material, not obstacle, not tool. The other is a focus whose own valid continuation is constitutive of mine.

In light of §27, 仁 admits a finer characterization: it is the *navigation polarity within the good interval*. Knowing when to rest (toward 平) and when to engage (toward 和) — both within the open-preserving region — is 仁's practical work. The two poles are not good-vs-bad but two modes of good; 仁 is the wisdom to oscillate appropriately between them in response to field state.

#### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{仁}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ acts under recognition that } \text{续}(a) \text{ supervenes on } \text{共续}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; a \text{ selects } i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \text{ preserving } \text{共开}_F\;}
$$

equivalently:

$$
\text{仁}_F \;\;:\equiv\;\; \text{己续实现于共场之续}
$$

**As intra-interval polarity navigation** (refining via §27):

$$
\text{仁}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ keeps } \big(\Gamma,\, \Gamma \oplus_F i\big) \text{ within } [\text{平},\,\text{和}] \text{ for all relevant scales}
$$

— 仁 is the disposition to remain in the good interval, including its calm pole.

> 仁 = 知己之续在共场之续中；亦平和区间之活住。

#### 合义

**仁是善在关系中之最高形式**。事见二开不相闭，物见 relational care, 场见 self-continuation through co-continuation, 亦见 [平, 和] 区间之内住。仁非为他者牺牲己——仁是**识己之续之真位，且知此位之活把握之对偶**。

### 三十三、义

#### 间相

义者，**此机中护开之当行**也。仁为关系之本，义为机中之断。无仁之义，易成刚暴；无义之仁，易成软弱而纵闭。

> **义者，临机择间之当也。**

#### 物相

**义 is the field-fitting selection, at a ji-moment, of the interval that best preserves co-openness under the prevailing scale, horizon, and calibration constraints.**

义 is not abstract rule-following — that is closure-by-form. It is also not arbitrary preference — that is closure-by-self. 义 is the specific intervention that fits *this* threshold of *this* configuration: what this object can bear, what this relation needs, what this field can become. Its rightness is contextual but constrained by structural criteria; arbitrariness is excluded.

#### 场相

$$
\boxed{\;\text{义}(a, \Gamma, i) \;\iff\; \text{机}(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \;\wedge\; i \in \arg\!\max_{j \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma)} \text{共开}_F\big(\Gamma \oplus_F j\big)\;\Big|_{\,(s, t),\,\text{四校}_F}\;}
$$

> 义 = 机中择其当间，以护共开。

#### 合义

义是仁入行动之断。事见当行，物见 fitting intervention, 场见 threshold interval selection。**仁定向，义断行**。

### 三十四、礼

礼者，**开之可传形式**。一境中之善行，若可重复、可教、可承、可使后来者易入其道，则成礼。礼活，则导人入境；礼死，则执形闭开。

$$
\text{礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; r \text{ encodes interval-selection patterns that preserve Open}_F \,\wedge\, r \text{ is transmissible across } \Gamma\text{-contexts}
$$

$$
\text{活礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{礼}(r, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{四校}_F(r, \Gamma)
$$

$$
\text{死礼}(r, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{形存}(r) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{应}_F(r, \Gamma)
$$

> **礼者，善间之可传也；气有渠而仍能应境。**

礼即第十四节 $\text{正筛}_F$ 之具体形——**礼是经多代受校之 selection-shaping 工具**。法律、协议、文化、教育、市场设计、algorithm alignment 皆可视为礼之诸形。礼之死，乃 selection field 失其 calibration 之能。

### 三十五、智

智者，**识开闭之能**。智非多知，非巧算。能识间、察机、辨伪开、知护闭、明尺度、受四校者，乃智。

$$
\boxed{\;\text{智}_F(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; a \text{ can evaluate } i \in \text{间}_F(\Gamma) \text{ along all axes:}\;}
$$

$$
\Big\{\,\text{局部效},\, \text{关系效},\, \text{结构效},\, \text{元效},\, \text{尺度校},\, \text{时窗校},\, \text{他者校},\, \text{伪开校},\, \text{四校}_F\,\Big\}
$$

> **智者，见间之开闭而不惑于一端也；气自知其通塞。**

智读 affordance-profile $I(a, \Gamma)$——见物可成何，可承何，何可护，何可夺，何似助而暗闭。**智是护开之眼**。

### 三十六、信

信者，**言行识感积相通而不自欺**。信非不变。变而有理，乃信；守旧而自欺，亦失信。信者，使内外诸开贯通，不令一处成伪。

$$
\text{信}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{贯}\Big(\text{言}_a,\, \text{行}_a,\, \text{模}_a,\, \text{感}_a,\, \mathcal{A}_a,\, \text{诺}_a\Big)
$$

$$
\boxed{\;\text{活信}(a, \Gamma) \;\iff\; \text{信}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \text{可修}(a, \Gamma) \,\wedge\, \neg\,\text{自欺}(a, \Gamma)\;}
$$

> **信者，诸间内通而不自裂；气贯言行识感积而不伪。**

仁定向，义断行，礼传善，智识开闭，**信使诸者内贯**。无信则四者皆可被窃为表演——故信为五常之内在条件。
